## Prompt 1: 温馨睡前故事

> 适合3-6岁幼儿的睡前故事绘本插画

**Prompt:**

```text
A children's picture book illustration for a bedtime story, double-page spread format (landscape). Scene: a small bear cub in striped pajamas being tucked into bed by mama bear in a cozy tree-house bedroom. Warm amber nightlight casting soft shadows, a window showing a crescent moon and twinkling stars outside. Details: a well-loved stuffed rabbit toy tucked under the blanket, a stack of storybooks on the nightstand, honey pot lamp, acorn decorations on the wooden walls. Art style: soft watercolor with visible paper texture, gentle rounded shapes with no sharp edges, warm color palette (golden yellows, soft browns, cozy oranges, sleepy lavenders), simple readable composition for young viewers, characters with large friendly eyes and gentle smiles, nurturing safe atmosphere, age-appropriate for 3-6 years old, whimsical storybook quality reminiscent of classic children's literature
```

**示例效果：**

![温馨睡前故事](images/childrens-book-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1536×1024 | 跨页横版 |
| 风格 | Watercolor Storybook | 水彩绘本 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为小兔子数星星入睡的故事
- 换成月亮婆婆巡夜的奇幻场景
- 加入梦境世界的过渡画面（从床飘向云端）

**标签**: `#comic` `#childrens-book` `#bedtime` `#watercolor` `#toddler`

## Prompt 2: 经典童话新绘

> 用现代插画风格重新诠释经典童话

**Prompt:**

```text
A modern children's book illustration reimagining "Jack and the Beanstalk", single page composition. A brave small boy (diverse representation, curly dark hair, bright red jacket) climbing an enormous twisting beanstalk that spirals up through fluffy clouds. The beanstalk is lush with oversized magical leaves in various greens, with tiny glowing fireflies and ladybugs living among the foliage. Far below, his tiny cottage and patchwork farmland are visible. Above, golden light breaks through clouds hinting at the giant's castle. Art style: modern digital illustration with bold graphic shapes and strong silhouettes, rich saturated colors with a palette of emerald greens, sky blues, golden yellows, and earthy browns, slightly textured flat color areas (grain texture overlay), dynamic diagonal composition creating sense of height and adventure, age-appropriate 4-8 years, inspired by contemporary award-winning picture books (Caldecott Medal quality), sense of wonder and courage
```

**示例效果：**

![经典童话新绘](images/childrens-book-02.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 方形单页 |
| 风格 | Modern Digital Illustration | 现代数字插画 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为小红帽（友善的狼版本，现代解读）
- 换成三只小猪的建筑工程师版
- 设计灰姑娘的太空版变体

**标签**: `#comic` `#childrens-book` `#fairy-tale` `#classic` `#modern-retelling`

## Prompt 3: 科学教育绘本

> STEM教育主题的趣味知识绘本

**Prompt:**

```text
A STEM educational picture book illustration about the water cycle for children ages 5-8. The scene shows the complete water cycle as a friendly journey: a cheerful water droplet character (with a face and tiny arms) starting in the ocean waving goodbye, then shown evaporating upward as steam with happy expression, forming into a cloud with other droplet friends (group hug forming a cumulus cloud), falling as rain with an excited "wheee!" expression, landing in a river, and flowing back to the ocean completing the cycle. The landscape includes mountains, a river, ocean, and sky all in one panoramic view. Labels in a child-friendly font: "蒸发 Evaporation", "凝结 Condensation", "降水 Precipitation", "汇流 Collection". Art style: bright cheerful colors (ocean blues, grass greens, sunny yellows), friendly rounded cartoon style, educational but never boring, clear visual flow with dotted path arrows showing the cycle, detailed enough to reward exploration but not overwhelming, playful infographic meets storybook aesthetic
```

**示例效果：**

![科学教育绘本](images/childrens-book-03.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1536×1024 | 横版教学展开 |
| 风格 | Educational Cartoon | 教育卡通 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为太阳系行星拟人化介绍
- 换成植物种子发芽成长过程
- 设计人体消化系统的"食物历险记"

**标签**: `#comic` `#childrens-book` `#education` `#STEM` `#science` `#water-cycle`

## Prompt 4: 剪纸拼贴风绘本

> 手工剪纸/拼贴艺术风格的绘本

**Prompt:**

```text
A children's picture book illustration in cut-paper collage style (inspired by Eric Carle and Lois Ehlert), depicting a colorful caterpillar's day in the garden. The scene shows a bright garden with oversized flowers (sunflowers, daisies, tulips) made from torn and cut textured papers - visible paper grain, tissue paper translucency, newspaper fragments, and painted paper textures. The caterpillar is made of overlapping circles in rainbow gradient colors, each segment a different textured paper. A smiling sun made of yellow and orange torn paper pieces, blue sky with cotton-like cloud textures, green grass made of layered cut strips. Art style: distinctly handcrafted collage aesthetic with visible edges and layering, rich tactile textures throughout, bold simple shapes that children could attempt to recreate with paper and glue, primary and secondary colors used boldly, white page visible between elements giving breathing room, joyful and craft-inspired, suitable for ages 2-5, sensory-rich visual experience
```

## Prompt 5: 中国风儿童绘本

> 融合中国传统美学的现代儿童绘本

**Prompt:**

```text
A Chinese-style children's picture book illustration telling the story of the Mid-Autumn Festival for children ages 4-8. Scene: a warm family gathering in a traditional courtyard (四合院) under a giant luminous full moon. A grandmother sits with two children at a round table with mooncakes, pomelos, and tea, telling the story of Chang'e. Above the scene, the legend comes alive in the clouds: a graceful Chang'e figure floating toward the moon with her jade rabbit, rendered in a dreamlike translucent style as if made of moonlight. The courtyard has red lanterns hanging, osmanthus tree with golden flowers, and a playful cat watching from the roof tiles. Art style: blending traditional Chinese painting elements (ink wash clouds, brushstroke textures) with modern children's book warmth and accessibility, color palette of moonlit silvers, warm lantern reds, osmanthus golds, and night sky deep blues, decorative borders with traditional cloud patterns (祥云), round composition echoing the moon/reunion theme (团圆), culturally authentic details presented in a child-friendly non-intimidating way
```

**示例效果：**

![中国风儿童绘本](images/childrens-book-05.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 方形绘本页 |
| 风格 | Chinese Modern Picture Book | 中国风现代绘本 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为春节主题（年兽的友善版故事）
- 换成端午节的龙舟和粽子
- 设计十二生肖的可爱动物运动会
