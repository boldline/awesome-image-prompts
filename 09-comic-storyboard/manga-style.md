## Prompt 1: 少年漫画动作场景

> 热血少年漫画风格的战斗分镜页

**Prompt:**

```text
A manga page layout in classic shounen action style (like Dragon Ball or Naruto), black and white with screen tones, dynamic multi-panel composition with one large splash panel dominating the page. The splash panel shows a young male protagonist mid-punch with extreme foreshortening, fist coming toward the viewer. Surrounding smaller panels show: reaction shots with speed lines, an opponent being knocked back with impact burst effects (star-shaped), motion blur and debris particles. Heavy use of speed lines (集中線), impact marks, dramatic diagonal panel borders breaking the grid, screen tone shading on hair and clothing, bold confident ink lines varying from thick to thin, Japanese manga page reading flow (right to left)
```

**示例效果：**

![少年漫画动作场景](images/manga-style-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 768×1024 | B5 漫画比例 |
| 风格 | Manga / Black & White | 黑白漫画 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 将拳击换为剑术对决，增加斩击特效
- 加入能量爆发/气场觉醒的经典桥段
- 改为多人混战的群像构图

**标签**: `#comic` `#manga` `#shounen` `#action` `#battle`

## Prompt 2: 少女漫画浪漫一幕

> 少女漫画风格的心动告白场景

**Prompt:**

```text
A shoujo manga page depicting a romantic confession scene, delicate and emotional art style. Main panel: a handsome boy reaching out his hand toward the viewer/heroine under cherry blossom trees, petals floating in the air, his expression gentle and earnest. Surrounding decorative panels: close-up of hands almost touching, heroine's blushing face with sparkly eyes (starlight reflections in large detailed eyes), heartbeat sound effect text (ドキドキ). Art style features: thin elegant line work, heavy use of floral screentone backgrounds, sparkles and bubbles floating in the atmosphere, soft halftone shading, elongated elegant figure proportions, decorative panel borders with flower motifs, emotional white space usage, black and white with gray tones
```

## Prompt 3: Q版/SD角色漫画

> Chibi 超变形可爱搞笑短漫

**Prompt:**

```text
A chibi/super-deformed (SD) manga comic page with 4-6 panels, characters drawn in 2-3 head tall proportions with oversized heads and tiny bodies. Scene: a group of chibi fantasy adventurers (warrior, mage, healer, thief) arguing over treasure distribution. Expressions are wildly exaggerated: anger veins (💢), sweat drops, blank white eyes for shock, cat-mouth (ω) expressions, steam coming from ears. Comic effects: oversized mallets for comedic hits, simplified backgrounds replaced with pattern fills (crosshatch, dots, stripes), comedic violence with bandages appearing instantly. Cute rounded line art, screen tone patterns, Japanese gag manga style, maximum expressiveness with minimal detail, chibi proportions throughout
```

**示例效果：**

![Q版角色搞笑漫画](images/manga-style-03.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |

| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 方形搞笑格式 |
| 风格 | Chibi / SD Manga | Q版漫画 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为日常学园喜剧主题
- 换成偶像团体后台的搞笑互动
- 用 Chibi 版知名角色做 crossover

**标签**: `#comic` `#manga` `#chibi` `#SD` `#comedy` `#cute`

## Prompt 4: 青年漫画写实风

> 成熟写实的青年漫画（Seinen）风格分镜

**Prompt:**

```text
A seinen manga page with mature, realistic art style reminiscent of Vagabond or Vinland Saga. A samurai warrior standing alone on a misty battlefield at dawn, detailed ink wash rendering of his weathered face and armor. Panel layout: tall vertical panel on the left showing full figure, three horizontal panels on the right showing close-ups - eyes with intense determination, hand gripping katana handle with visible calluses and scars, bare feet on blood-stained ground. Art technique: detailed cross-hatching for texture, ink wash effects for atmospheric depth, minimal screen tone replaced by hand-drawn shading, anatomically precise figure work, highly detailed fabric folds and environmental textures, cinematic composition with dramatic negative space, black and white with masterful gray gradients achieved through ink techniques
```

| 风格 | Seinen / Realistic Manga | 青年写实 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为现代都市悬疑题材（侦探、黑暗小巷）

## Prompt 5: 治愈日常漫画

> 温馨治愈的日常系漫画页面

**Prompt:**

```text
A slice-of-life manga page in the style of Yotsuba&! or Aria, warm and healing atmosphere. Scene: a young girl discovering a hidden garden behind an old bookstore on a lazy summer afternoon. Panel composition: establishing shot of a quiet Japanese shopping street with cicada sound effects (ミーンミーン), medium shot of the girl peeking through an ivy-covered gate with curiosity, wide panel revealing a lush secret garden with a cat napping on a bench and sunlight filtering through leaves, close-up of her amazed smile with sparkly eyes. Art style: clean minimal lines with selective detail (detailed backgrounds, simpler characters), light delicate screen tones, warm atmosphere conveyed through careful use of white space, gentle pacing with no rush, nature details (leaves, flowers, insects) adding life to every panel, peaceful contemplative mood
```

| 风格 | Slice-of-life Manga | 日常治愈系 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**
