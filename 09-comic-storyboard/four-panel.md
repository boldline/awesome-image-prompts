## Prompt 1: 日常生活幽默四格

> 以办公室白领为主角的日常搞笑四格漫画

**Prompt:**

```text
A 4-panel comic strip in 2x2 grid layout depicting a humorous office life scenario: Panel 1 (setup) - an enthusiastic employee arrives at work with coffee, bright expression; Panel 2 (development) - opens laptop to find 99+ unread emails; Panel 3 (twist) - starts replying frantically with multiple arms like an octopus; Panel 4 (punchline) - realizes it's Saturday, deadpan expression. Consistent character design across all panels, bold clean outlines, pastel color palette, expressive cartoon style, speech bubbles with short text, each panel clearly bordered with rounded corners, white background
```

**示例效果：**

![日常办公幽默四格漫画](images/four-panel-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 2x2 方形布局 |
| 风格 | Cartoon / Comic | 卡通漫画 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 将办公室场景换为居家远程办公
- 改为程序员 debug 场景（发现 bug → 修复 → 产生更多 bug → 关机下班）
- 增加同事角色形成互动喜剧

**标签**: `#comic` `#four-panel` `#office` `#humor`

## Prompt 2: 猫咪宠物四格

> 铲屎官与猫咪的日常互动趣事

**Prompt:**

```text
A vertical 4-panel comic strip (4コマ style, arranged top to bottom) about a cat owner's daily life: Panel 1 - owner carefully prepares an expensive gourmet cat meal in a fancy bowl; Panel 2 - presents the food to a fluffy orange tabby cat with hopeful expression; Panel 3 - cat sniffs the food, turns away with disdainful expression; Panel 4 - cat is happily eating a cardboard box instead while owner collapses in despair. Cute rounded art style, warm colors, consistent character proportions, thick outlines, manga-influenced expressions, light pink and cream background tones, numbered panels with thin borders
```

**示例效果：**

![猫咪宠物趣事四格](images/four-panel-02.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 768×1024 | 竖版四格排列 |
| 风格 | Cute Cartoon | 可爱卡通 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 将猫换为哈士奇（拆家主题）
- 多猫家庭版本（猫咪社交等级）
- 加入猫咪内心OS对话气泡

**标签**: `#comic` `#four-panel` `#cat` `#pet` `#cute`

## Prompt 3: 科普教学四格

> 用四格漫画解释一个科学知识点

**Prompt:**

```text
A 4-panel educational comic strip in 2x2 grid explaining why the sky is blue: Panel 1 - a curious child character with a magnifying glass asks "Why is the sky blue?" with a thought bubble; Panel 2 - diagram showing white sunlight hitting Earth's atmosphere, with labeled arrows; Panel 3 - blue light scattering in all directions (Rayleigh scattering) visualized as bouncing blue dots, while red/yellow passes through; Panel 4 - child character on a hill at sunset saying "So at sunset, blue scatters away and we see red!" with an orange sky behind. Clean infographic-meets-comic style, educational yet fun, bright primary colors, simple character design, labeled diagrams within panels, clear readable layout
```

**示例效果：**

![科普教学四格漫画](images/four-panel-03.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 2x2 教学布局 |
| 风格 | Educational Illustration | 科普插画 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为解释"为什么船浮在水上"（阿基米德原理）
- 生物主题：细胞分裂过程
- 用拟人化分子/原子作为角色

**标签**: `#comic` `#four-panel` `#education` `#science`

## Prompt 4: 职场吐槽四格

> 打工人心声系列——会议主题

**Prompt:**

```text
A 4-panel comic strip about endless work meetings, satirical office humor: Panel 1 - a calendar notification pops up "Meeting 3:00 PM - Quick Sync" and the protagonist looks relieved thinking it will be short; Panel 2 - meeting room scene at 3:00, manager says "Just a few updates..." with 47 slides visible on the screen; Panel 3 - clock shows 5:30 PM, the protagonist's soul is visibly leaving their body (ghost floating up) while the manager says "One more thing..."; Panel 4 - skeleton sitting in the chair, cobwebs everywhere, slides show "Page 2 of 47". Corporate Memphis adjacent flat illustration style, muted office color palette with pops of humor, exaggerated expressions, clean vector-like aesthetic, subtle visual gags in background
```

**示例效果：**

![职场吐槽四格漫画](images/four-panel-04.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 2x2 标准布局 |
| 风格 | Flat Illustration / Comic | 扁平插画 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为"这个需求很简单"系列
- 换成远程会议版本（摄像头关闭、静音吃零食）
- 增加多人同事的集体反应

**标签**: `#comic` `#four-panel` `#office` `#satire` `#workplace`

## Prompt 5: 情侣日常甜蜜四格

> 恋人之间的小温馨小甜蜜瞬间

**Prompt:**

```text
A 4-panel romantic comedy comic strip about a couple's daily life: Panel 1 - boyfriend attempts to cook a surprise dinner, kitchen is slightly chaotic with smoke rising; Panel 2 - girlfriend comes home, sees the mess, boyfriend nervously presenting a burnt dish with a hopeful smile; Panel 3 - close-up of the plate showing an unrecognizable charred item shaped vaguely like a heart; Panel 4 - both laughing together while ordering delivery on the phone, the burnt heart-dish displayed proudly on the table like a trophy. Soft pastel watercolor comic style, warm pink and peach tones, gentle line art, shoujo manga influence with sparkle effects and flower petals, rounded panel borders, dreamy atmosphere
```

**示例效果：**

![情侣日常甜蜜四格](images/four-panel-05.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 2x2 方形布局 |
| 风格 | Soft Watercolor Comic | 柔和水彩漫画 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为异地恋视频通话场景
- 换成纪念日惊喜翻车系列
- 季节限定版：雪天/雨天/樱花季
