## Prompt 1: 都市恋爱条漫

> 甜蜜校园/都市恋爱题材的韩式条漫

**Prompt:**

```text
A webtoon vertical scroll comic panel sequence for a modern romance story, 4 connected panels in tall portrait format optimized for mobile reading. Scene: a coffee shop meet-cute moment. Panel 1 (wide): cozy café interior with warm lighting, bookshelves and plants, the female lead reading at a window seat; Panel 2 (medium): male lead accidentally bumps her table while passing, her coffee wobbles dangerously; Panel 3 (close-up): their eyes meet for the first time - his apologetic, hers surprised - sparkle effects and soft pink overlay background replacing the café; Panel 4 (detail): his hand steadying her coffee cup, their fingers almost touching, time seems frozen with floating particles. Korean webtoon art style: clean digital linework, soft cel-shading with gradient shadows, pastel color palette (blush pink, warm cream, soft brown), detailed character designs with fashionable modern clothing, expressive large eyes with detailed highlights, seamless panel transitions flowing into each other without hard borders, atmospheric lighting effects
```

**示例效果：**

![都市恋爱条漫](images/webtoon-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 768×1024 | 竖屏滚动格式 |
| 风格 | Korean Webtoon / Romance | 韩漫恋爱 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为办公室暗恋题材（电梯偶遇）
- 换成青梅竹马重逢场景
- 加入雨天共伞的经典桥段

**标签**: `#comic` `#webtoon` `#romance` `#modern` `#korean`

## Prompt 2: 奇幻冒险条漫

> 异世界/奇幻题材的华丽条漫画面

Panel 1 (establishing): a dark ancient temple interior with glowing runes on the walls, the young protagonist (silver-haired, cloaked) standing before a sealed door, mysterious atmosphere; Panel 2 (action): the door begins cracking with blinding light seeping through, protagonist shielding their eyes, energy tendrils reaching toward them; Panel 3 (transformation - largest panel): protagonist floating mid-air, eyes glowing with magical energy, ancient armor/marks materializing on their body, massive magical circle expanding beneath, dramatic upward camera angle; Panel 4 (reveal): full awakened form revealed in a heroic pose with elemental energy swirling, the sealed door now shattered revealing a vast landscape beyond. Rich saturated color palette (deep purples, magical golds, ethereal blues), detailed fantasy environment design, dynamic lighting with multiple light sources, particle effects and magical VFX, Korean webtoon polish with painterly rendering in key moments, borderless panels blending into each other with energy effects bridging transitions

## Prompt 3: 悬疑惊悚条漫

> 恐怖/悬疑题材的氛围感条漫

**Prompt:**

```text
A horror/thriller webtoon panel sequence, 5 vertical panels building suspense for mobile reading. Scene: a person realizes they are not alone in their apartment. Panel 1 (calm): mundane evening scene, protagonist on couch watching TV in a dimly lit living room, normal atmosphere; Panel 2 (unease): same scene but something is slightly off - in a mirror on the wall, there appears to be an extra shadow that doesn't match anything in the room; Panel 3 (tension): close-up of protagonist's face as they slowly notice something, eyes shifting to the side, cold sweat on temple; Panel 4 (dread): protagonist's POV looking down a dark hallway, at the far end a door that should be closed is slightly ajar, a sliver of darkness visible, the hallway seems unnaturally long; Panel 5 (reveal - black panel with minimal elements): all lights are off, only the protagonist's terrified white eyes visible in the darkness, and behind them - two more eyes, uncomfortably close, smiling. Color palette shifts from warm normal tones in panel 1 to progressively colder, desaturated, sickly greens by panel 5. Strategic use of black negative space, uncomfortable panel proportions (progressively taller/narrower), inspired by Chiller/Tales of the Unusual webtoon horror aesthetics
```

**示例效果：**

![悬疑惊悚条漫](images/webtoon-03.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 768×1024 | 竖屏滚动格式 |
| 风格 | Horror Webtoon | 恐怖条漫 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为电梯里多出来一个人的城市怪谈
- 换成照片中发现不该存在的面孔
- 设计互动式"向下滚动揭示"的惊吓效果

**标签**: `#comic` `#webtoon` `#horror` `#thriller` `#suspense`

## Prompt 4: 日常搞笑条漫

> 轻松搞笑的短篇日常条漫

**Prompt:**

```text
A comedic slice-of-life webtoon strip, 4 panels in vertical scroll format, light-hearted and relatable humor. Scene: the stages of setting an alarm for the morning. Panel 1: character confidently setting 5 alarms on their phone at night (5:00, 5:05, 5:10, 5:15, 5:20), determined expression, speech bubble "Tomorrow I WILL wake up early!"; Panel 2: morning - phone showing 5:00 AM alarm ringing with intense visual effects, character as a cocooned blanket burrito, one hand blindly slapping at phone, eyes firmly shut; Panel 3: montage of all 5 alarms being dismissed in increasingly creative unconscious ways (foot, face-plant on phone, throwing pillow at it), time now shows 5:20; Panel 4: character suddenly bolt upright in bed with shocked face, clock shows 8:45 AM, phone shows "boss_calling.jpg", pure panic with speed lines and alarm symbols everywhere. Bright cheerful color palette even in the disaster panel, cute simplified character design (2.5-3 heads tall), exaggerated expressions with emoji-like faces (>_<, @_@, !!!), clean flat coloring with minimal shading, relatable everyday comedy style, SNS/social media friendly format
```

## Prompt 5: 武侠/仙侠条漫

> 中国风武侠仙侠题材的国漫条漫

**Prompt:**

```text
A Chinese martial arts/xianxia (cultivation) webtoon panel sequence, 4 vertical panels with cinematic wuxia aesthetics. Scene: a sword cultivator's breakthrough moment on a mountain peak. Panel 1 (atmosphere): sweeping landscape of impossibly tall misty mountains with waterfalls, tiny figure meditating on a cliff edge, ink wash background with color accents, traditional Chinese painting influence; Panel 2 (buildup): close-up of the cultivator, long flowing hair and white/blue traditional hanfu robes billowing, spiritual energy (qi) visible as luminous threads converging, lotus flowers blooming in the air around them; Panel 3 (climax - largest panel): the cultivator draws their sword in a single fluid motion, a crescent slash of golden energy cuts across the sky parting the clouds, the sword trail leaves glowing Chinese calligraphy characters in the air, dramatic perspective from below; Panel 4 (aftermath): wide shot of the mountain now cleared of mist, the cultivator floating on their sword above the clouds, sunrise breaking through in golden rays, celestial crane flying alongside. Art style blending Chinese ink painting aesthetics with modern digital webtoon polish, color palette of ink blacks, jade greens, cloud whites, and spiritual golds, flowing dynamic lines for energy and movement, traditional pattern motifs in clothing and magical effects
```

**示例效果：**

![武侠仙侠条漫](images/webtoon-05.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 768×1024 | 竖屏滚动格式 |
| 风格 | Xianxia / Chinese Fantasy Webtoon | 仙侠国漫 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为现代都市修仙（地铁里渡劫）
