## Prompt 1: 超级英雄动态封面

> 经典超级英雄漫画封面构图

**Prompt:**

```text
A dynamic superhero comic book cover in classic Marvel/DC style, a caped hero hovering above a nighttime cityscape, dramatic low-angle perspective looking up at the figure silhouetted against a full moon. Bold composition with the hero's cape billowing in the wind, one fist raised forward in a classic heroic pose. City buildings below rendered in detailed perspective with lit windows. Art style: bold ink outlines with confident thick-to-thin brushwork, cross-hatching for shadows on the costume, vibrant saturated 4-color comic printing aesthetic (CMYK dots visible at close range), dramatic rim lighting in electric blue, strong shadows with minimal midtones, title banner area at top, issue number and Comics Code Authority seal placement, classic newsprint color palette with bold primaries
```

**示例效果：**

![超级英雄动态封面](images/western-comic-01.png)

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |

| 尺寸 | 768×1024 | 美漫封面比例 |
| 风格 | Superhero Comic | 超英漫画 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为团队集结封面（复仇者联盟式群像）
- 换成反派主题的黑暗封面
- 加入经典漫画标题字体和价格标签

**标签**: `#comic` `#western-comic` `#superhero` `#cover` `#marvel`

## Prompt 2: 独立漫画 Graphic Novel

> 文学性强的独立图像小说风格

**Prompt:**

```text
A graphic novel page in the style of indie comics (reminiscent of Daniel Clowes or Chris Ware), introspective and literary. Scene: a lonely figure sitting at a diner counter at 2 AM, viewed from outside through a rain-streaked window. Panel layout: precise geometric grid, 3 rows of panels with mathematical precision. Color palette: limited to 3-4 muted colors (dusty blue, warm yellow from diner lights, gray, muted red), flat color areas with no gradients. Art style: clean precise line work, architectural attention to perspective and spatial detail, mundane objects drawn with care (coffee cup, napkin holder, counter texture), emotional storytelling through composition and color rather than facial expression, Edward Hopper meets comic art, melancholic atmosphere, sophisticated typography for any text elements
```

**示例效果：**

![独立漫画图像小说](images/western-comic-02.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 768×1024 | 图像小说比例 |
| 风格 | Indie Graphic Novel | 独立图像小说 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为城市公寓楼的多角色并行叙事
- 换成回忆与现实交织的双色调对比
- 尝试无文字纯视觉叙事页面

**标签**: `#comic` `#western-comic` `#graphic-novel` `#indie` `#literary`

## Prompt 3: 复古纸浆杂志风

> 1940s-50s 纸浆小说/黑色电影漫画封面

**Prompt:**

```text
A pulp fiction comic cover from the 1940s-1950s era, noir detective genre. A hardboiled private investigator in a fedora and trench coat stands in a rain-soaked alley, holding a smoking revolver, a femme fatale silhouette visible in a doorway behind him casting long shadows from a single streetlamp. Art style: painted illustration technique (gouache/oil feel), dramatic chiaroscuro lighting with deep blacks and limited warm highlights, slightly oversaturated pulp coloring (deep reds, moody blues, sickly yellows), visible brushwork texture suggesting traditional painting, sensationalist cover composition, vintage aged paper texture and yellowing effect, retro typography for title "MIDNIGHT DETECTIVE" in bold distressed serif font, 10-cent price tag in corner, worn edges and printing imperfections
```

## Prompt 4: 现代美漫内页分镜

> 当代美漫的动态内页布局

**Prompt:**

```text
A modern American comic book interior page with dynamic panel layout, contemporary superhero action sequence. A speedster hero racing through city streets saving civilians from an explosion. Panel arrangement: dramatic diagonal panel borders breaking the traditional grid, one panel bleeds off the page edge, inset panels overlap larger panels for pacing. Specific panels: wide establishing shot of explosion, three rapid-fire small panels showing the hero's movement as a blur saving different people, large panel showing the hero skidding to a stop with debris settling. Art style: modern digital inking with clean consistent line weights, full digital coloring with rendering and light effects (lens flares, glowing energy), cinematic color grading (warm explosion tones vs. cool speed blur), motion blur effects on the speedster, debris and particle effects, modern mainstream comics production quality (Jim Lee meets Ivan Reis aesthetic)
```

**示例效果：**

![现代美漫内页分镜](images/western-comic-04.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 768×1024 | 标准美漫页 |
| 风格 | Modern Superhero Comic | 现代超英内页 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为太空战斗场景（星际战舰对决）
- 换成街头格斗风格（无超能力写实打斗）
- 调整为对话密集的剧情页（多文字气泡布局）

**标签**: `#comic` `#western-comic` `#superhero` `#action` `#modern`

## Prompt 5: 欧洲 BD 风格漫画

> 法比风格的欧洲漫画（Bande Dessinée）

**Prompt:**

```text
A European comic (Franco-Belgian Bande Dessinée) page in the style of Moebius/Jean Giraud or Hergé's ligne claire. A science fiction scene: an explorer in a retro-futuristic spacesuit walking across an alien desert landscape with bizarre organic rock formations and two suns in the sky. Panel layout: clean rectangular grid with generous gutters, 3 rows with 2-3 panels each, orderly and readable. Art style: ligne claire (clear line) technique with uniform line weight throughout, no cross-hatching, flat colors with subtle limited palette (desert ochres, alien purples, sky gradients), meticulous environmental detail in every panel, architectural precision in mechanical/suit design, European album format quality (48-page hardcover aesthetic), contemplative pacing with wide landscape panels, watercolor-like coloring with soft edges within hard line boundaries
```

**示例效果：**

![欧洲BD风格漫画](images/western-comic-05.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 欧漫大开本 |
| 风格 | Ligne Claire / BD | 欧洲清线风 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为丁丁历险记风格的冒险故事
- 换成蒸汽朋克巴黎的都市幻想
- 尝试 Moebius 的迷幻沙漠世界观

**标签**: `#comic` `#western-comic` `#BD` `#european` `#ligne-claire` `#moebius`
