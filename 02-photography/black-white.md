## Prompt 1: 高对比街头

> 强烈明暗对比的黑白街头摄影，光影中的决定性瞬间

**Prompt:**

```text
A dramatic high-contrast black and white street photograph,
deep blacks and bright whites with minimal midtones,
strong directional sunlight creating graphic shadows on architecture,
lone figure walking through a beam of light between buildings,
shot on Leica M with 50mm Summilux wide open,
Tri-X 400 film grain texture and tonal response,
Henri Cartier-Bresson decisive moment aesthetic,
shadow patterns from wrought iron railings or window blinds cutting across the scene,
precise geometric composition using light and dark zones,
gallery-worthy fine art black and white photography
```

**示例效果：**

![高对比街头](images/black-white-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 方形或竖版 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 黑白艺术 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为雨天街头，水面反射增加光影层次
- 加入多人场景，剪影群像在逆光中
- 替换为楼梯或走廊的几何光影图案

**标签**: `#photography` `#black-white` `#street` `#contrast` `#decisive-moment`

## Prompt 2: 黑白人像

> 经典黑白人像摄影，用光影雕刻面部的每一个轮廓

**Prompt:**

```text
A powerful black and white portrait photograph with dramatic studio lighting,
single strong directional light source from 45 degrees creating Rembrandt lighting,
deep shadows sculpting the facial bone structure and contours,
catchlight in the eyes providing life and connection with the viewer,
subject: elderly person with character lines and textured skin telling a life story,
every wrinkle and pore rendered in exquisite detail,
dark background fading to pure black for complete subject isolation,
shot at 85mm f/2 with razor-thin focus plane on the near eye,
silver gelatin print tonal range from deep black to paper white,
subtle film grain adding organic texture throughout,
Irving Penn and Richard Avedon inspired timeless portrait aesthetic,
museum-quality fine art black and white portraiture
```

**示例效果：**

![黑白人像](images/black-white-02.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1536 | 竖版人像构图 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 艺术肖像 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为高调人像，白色背景和柔和过曝
- 加入双重曝光效果，面部与城市景观融合
- 替换为侧面剪影，只保留轮廓线条

**标签**: `#photography` `#black-white` `#portrait` `#studio` `#dramatic`

> 壮阔的黑白风景摄影，用灰度表达自然的永恒力量

**Prompt:**

```text
A majestic black and white landscape photograph with epic scale,
long exposure of ocean waves crashing against rocky coastline,
silky smooth water from 30-second exposure creating ethereal mist effect,
dramatic storm clouds with complex tonal gradations from white to black,
lone weathered tree or lighthouse on cliff edge as focal point,
full tonal range utilized: zone system Zone 0 to Zone X,
strong foreground interest with textured rocks leading to middle ground,
ND filter long exposure turning choppy sea into calm glass,
Ansel Adams zone system approach to exposure and development,
large format 4x5 camera sharpness and tonal depth,
fine art landscape print quality suitable for large gallery display,
timeless and elemental quality transcending specific time or era
```

## Prompt 4: 黑白建筑

> 建筑的黑白几何抽象，线条与形状的纯粹视觉语言

**Prompt:**

```text
A striking black and white architectural photograph emphasizing pure geometry,
modern building facade reduced to abstract patterns of light and shadow,
repetitive structural elements: columns, arches, or window grids creating rhythm,
strong converging lines drawing the eye to a central vanishing point,
high contrast processing eliminating midtones for graphic bold impact,
spiral staircase or curved facade creating dynamic flowing lines,
play of positive and negative space between structure and sky,
symmetrical composition with mathematical precision,
architectural details rendered as abstract graphic art,
no people or distracting elements - pure form and structure,
shot on medium format for extreme resolution and tonal smoothness,
Gabriele Basilico and Lucien Hervé inspired architectural abstraction
```

## Prompt 5: 黑白抽象

> 日常物体的黑白抽象摄影，解构现实创造纯粹视觉体验

**Prompt:**

```text
An abstract black and white fine art photograph transforming everyday objects,
extreme close-up of organic or industrial forms reduced to shapes and tones,
crumpled fabric, smoke wisps, or liquid surfaces creating flowing abstract forms,
complete removal of context - the subject becomes unrecognizable texture,
full exploitation of black and white tonal range for maximum visual impact,
strong directional lighting creating dramatic chiaroscuro effect,
motion blur or intentional camera movement creating painterly streaks,
grain and texture from high ISO or darkroom techniques adding tactile quality,
the image exists purely as composition of light, shadow, form, and texture,
no literal representation - viewer creates their own meaning,
Aaron Siskind and Minor White inspired photographic abstraction,
museum-quality contemporary fine art photography
```

**示例效果：**

![黑白抽象](images/black-white-05.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 方形适合抽象 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 抽象艺术 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 替换为水面波纹的抽象光影图案
- 改为烟雾在黑色背景中的流动形态
- 加入多重曝光技法叠加不同纹理

**标签**: `#photography` `#black-white` `#abstract` `#fineart` `#texture`
