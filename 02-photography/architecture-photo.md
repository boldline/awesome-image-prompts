## Prompt 1: 现代摩天大楼

> 现代玻璃幕墙摩天大楼的极简几何美学

**Prompt:**

```text
A striking architectural photograph of a modern glass skyscraper,
looking up from ground level with dramatic converging vertical lines,
reflective glass curtain wall mirroring clouds and adjacent buildings,
strong geometric repetition of window panels creating visual rhythm,
late afternoon sun creating golden reflections on upper floors,
deep blue sky with wispy clouds as negative space,
shot with 24mm tilt-shift lens for controlled perspective,
limited color palette: steel blue glass, golden light, white clouds,
architectural photography award quality, precise composition
```

**示例效果：**

![现代摩天大楼](images/architecture-photo-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1536 | 竖版突出建筑高度 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 建筑摄影 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为双子塔对称构图，强调镜像效果
- 换成雨天拍摄，玻璃幕墙上的雨滴纹理
- 尝试夜景模式，室内灯光形成棋盘格图案

**标签**: `#photography` `#architecture` `#skyscraper` `#modern` `#geometric`

## Prompt 2: 粗野主义建筑

> 混凝土粗野主义建筑的力量感与纹理

**Prompt:**

```text
A powerful photograph of a Brutalist concrete architecture structure,
massive raw concrete forms with visible formwork texture and aggregate,
dramatic geometric cantilevers and deep recessed shadows,
overcast sky creating even diffused light on concrete surfaces,
strong interplay of positive and negative space,
human figure at base providing overwhelming scale reference,
monochromatic tonal range: concrete grays from near-white to deep charcoal,
symmetrical frontal composition emphasizing monumental presence,
Barbican Centre or Habitat 67 inspired aesthetic,
shot on medium format for maximum detail in concrete texture,
Hélène Binet architectural photography style
```

## Prompt 5: 桥梁工程之美

> 大型桥梁结构的工程美学与光影

**Prompt:**

```text
A dramatic architectural photograph of a monumental bridge structure,
suspension cables creating rhythmic diagonal lines converging to vanishing point,
shot from the pedestrian walkway perspective looking along the bridge span,
blue hour twilight sky with bridge lights just illuminating,
steel structure details: rivets, cables, and painted girders in sharp focus,
long exposure smoothing water surface below into silky mirror,
symmetrical composition centered on the bridge's central axis,
complementary color contrast: warm tungsten bridge lights against cool blue sky,
fog rolling beneath the bridge deck adding mystical depth,
fine art architectural photography quality,
inspired by Golden Gate or Brooklyn Bridge iconic compositions
```

**示例效果：**

![桥梁工程之美](images/architecture-photo-05.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1536×1024 | 横版展现桥梁跨度 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 建筑工程摄影 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**
