## Prompt 1: 超跑动态广告

> 超级跑车的动态摄影，展现速度与力量的完美瞬间

**Prompt:**

```text
A dynamic automotive photography shot of a luxury sports car,
three-quarter front view on a dramatic mountain pass with sweeping curves,
golden hour rim lighting creating specular highlights on the sculpted bodywork,
slight motion blur on wheels suggesting movement while body stays sharp,
rig shot perspective with motion-blurred background streaking past,
rich reflections on deep metallic paint surface in midnight blue,
low angle camera at bumper height emphasizing the aggressive wide stance,
visible heat distortion from exhaust area,
automotive magazine cover quality, Phase One IQ4 medium format clarity
```

**示例效果：**

![超跑动态广告](images/vehicle-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1536×1024 | 横版展现车身 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 汽车广告 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 将场景换为城市隧道，强调灯带拉丝效果
- 改为正侧面静态展示，突出车身线条设计
- 加入雨天湿地场景，轮胎激起水花

**标签**: `#photography` `#vehicle` `#sportscar` `#automotive`
