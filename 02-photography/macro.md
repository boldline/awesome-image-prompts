## Prompt 1: 水滴微观世界

> 水滴中的倒映世界，每一颗水珠都是一个微型透镜

**Prompt:**

```text
An extreme macro photograph of water droplets on a smooth surface,
individual water drops acting as tiny spherical lenses refracting the background,
a colorful flower or landscape scene visible inverted inside each droplet,
shot at 2:1 magnification with a dedicated macro lens and extension tubes,
focus stacking of 30+ frames for complete front-to-back sharpness,
creamy smooth bokeh in complementary colors behind the drops,
studio lighting with a small softbox creating perfect specular highlights,
surface tension visible at the base of each drop meeting the surface,
various droplet sizes from tiny beads to large dome-shaped drops,
scientific precision meets fine art photography aesthetic
```

**示例效果：**

![水滴微观世界](images/macro-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 方形突出主体 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 微距艺术 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 将背景替换为城市天际线在水滴中的倒影
- 改为雨后蜘蛛网上的水珠串联
- 加入水滴坠落瞬间的高速摄影效果

**标签**: `#photography` `#macro` `#waterdrops` `#refraction` `#abstract`

## Prompt 2: 昆虫肖像

> 昆虫面部的超微距特写，揭示肉眼不可见的精密结构

**Prompt:**

```text
An incredible super-macro portrait of an insect face in extreme detail,
a dragonfly or jumping spider head filling the entire frame,
compound eyes revealed as thousands of individual hexagonal ommatidia,
each facet reflecting a tiny point of light like a disco ball,
fine hairs and sensory structures visible on the head and mandibles,
shot at 5:1 magnification with microscope objective adapted to camera,
focus stacking of 100+ slices for impossible depth of field,
dark background isolating the subject completely,
ring flash providing even shadowless illumination of complex geometry,
iridescent structural colors on the exoskeleton: greens, blues, golds,
scientific illustration quality with fine art composition,
National Geographic macro photography award winning quality
```

**Prompt:**

```text
A breathtaking macro photograph exploring the interior structure of a flower,

pistil and stamen structures in sharp focus revealing reproductive geometry,
shot at 1:1 macro with soft directional backlight creating petal translucency,

rich saturated colors: deep crimson fading to blush pink at petal edges,
morning dew drops nestled in petal folds adding sparkle points,
fine art botanical photography bridging science and beauty,
```

> 电路板的微距摄影，硅基世界的赛博朋克地形图

**Prompt:**

