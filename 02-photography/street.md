## Prompt 1: 东京雨夜霓虹

> 雨夜东京街头的霓虹倒影与行人剪影

**Prompt:**

```text
A moody street photography shot of a bustling Tokyo street at night in the rain,
neon signs in Japanese kanji reflected in puddles on the wet pavement,
pedestrians with transparent umbrellas creating silhouettes against backlit storefronts,
shot at 35mm f/2 for environmental context with slight subject isolation,
high ISO grain adding to the atmosphere,
Saul Leiter inspired color and composition,
candid moment captured in passing,
rich contrast between warm neon pinks and cool rain-soaked blues
```

**示例效果：**

![东京雨夜霓虹街头](images/street-01.jpg)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1536 | 竖版更有街头感 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 纪实风格 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 将东京替换为大阪道顿堀，增加更密集的霓虹招牌
- 改为出租车视角，透过车窗雨滴拍摄外面的街景
- 聚焦单个撑伞行人，使用长焦压缩背景霓虹

**标签**: `#photography` `#street` `#tokyo` `#rain` `#neon`

## Prompt 2: 巴黎雨中街景

> 雨天巴黎街头的浪漫与诗意氛围

**Prompt:**

```text
A cinematic street photograph of a rainy Parisian boulevard,
cobblestone streets glistening with rain reflections,
a lone figure in a dark coat walking past a classic café with warm amber interior light spilling out,
Haussmann-style buildings receding into misty background,
shot at 50mm f/1.8 creating gentle bokeh on background lights,
muted color palette of slate gray, warm gold, and deep navy,
slight motion blur on passing pedestrians suggesting movement,
reminiscent of Brassai's Paris at night meets modern street photography
```

**示例效果：**

![巴黎雨中街景](images/street-02.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1536×1024 | 横版展现街道纵深 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 纪实偏电影感 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 加入红色雨伞作为画面视觉焦点
- 改为蒙马特小巷的台阶场景，增加层次感
- 尝试黄昏时刻，天空保留最后一丝蓝紫色

**标签**: `#photography` `#street` `#paris` `#rain` `#cinematic`

## Prompt 3: 纽约地铁瞬间

> 纽约地铁站内的人文纪实抓拍

**Prompt:**

```text
A raw candid street photograph inside a New York City subway station,
fluorescent and tungsten mixed lighting casting green-yellow tones on tiled walls,
a commuter standing alone on the platform reading a newspaper,
motion-blurred train arriving in the background with streaked lights,
gritty urban atmosphere with authentic NYC subway tile and signage,
shot at 28mm f/2.8 for wide environmental storytelling,
Bruce Davidson subway series inspired,
high contrast with crushed shadows and visible film grain,
authentic urban documentary photography feel
```

**示例效果：**

![纽约地铁瞬间](images/street-03.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1536×1024 | 横版适合站台场景 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 纪实粗粒感 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为车厢内部视角，乘客各自沉浸在手机中
- 加入街头艺人在站台演奏萨克斯的场景
- 用长曝光拍摄列车进站，人物保持静止形成对比

**标签**: `#photography` `#street` `#nyc` `#subway` `#documentary`

## Prompt 4: 亚洲夜市烟火

> 热闹夜市中的烟火气息与市井生活

**Prompt:**

```text
A vibrant street photograph of a bustling Asian night market,
steam rising from multiple food stalls creating atmospheric haze,
warm tungsten bulbs and colorful hand-painted signage,
vendor in motion cooking with flames leaping from a wok,
crowds of people navigating narrow lanes between stalls,
shot at 24mm f/2 capturing the overwhelming sensory environment,
rich saturated colors: red lanterns, golden oil, green vegetables,
shallow focus on foreground food preparation with bokeh crowd behind,
authentic night market energy and chaos captured in a single decisive moment
```

**示例效果：**

![亚洲夜市烟火](images/street-04.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1536 | 竖版突出层次 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 纪实暖色调 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 聚焦某个特定摊位的制作过程特写

**标签**: `#photography` `#street` `#nightmarket` `#asia` `#food`

## Prompt 5: 复古街区漫步

> 充满年代感的老城区街拍，怀旧与现代交织

**Prompt:**

```text
A nostalgic street photograph of a vintage neighborhood district,
faded painted shop signs and weathered wooden storefronts from decades past,
an elderly shopkeeper sitting outside reading a newspaper in afternoon light,
old bicycle leaning against a peeling paint wall,
dappled sunlight filtering through overhead tree canopy,
shot on vintage lens with characteristic swirly bokeh and warm color shift,
Kodak Portra 400 film simulation with soft pastel tones,
layers of time visible in architectural details and patina,
quiet contemplative moment in a rapidly changing urban landscape
```

**示例效果：**

![复古街区漫步](images/street-05.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1536 | 竖版适合巷弄纵深 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 胶片复古感 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为欧洲小镇的鹅卵石巷弄，加入晾晒的衣物
- 尝试日本昭和风商店街，加入自动贩卖机对比
- 改为雨后场景，潮湿路面反射老建筑的色彩

**标签**: `#photography` `#street` `#vintage` `#nostalgia` `#film`
