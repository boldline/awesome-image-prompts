## Prompt 1: 精致料理摆盘

> 米其林级别精致料理的商业摄影

**Prompt:**

```text
A professional food photography shot of a beautifully plated fine dining dish,
artistic sauce swooshes and microgreen garnishes on a white porcelain plate,
shot from 45-degree angle on a dark slate surface,
soft directional window light from the left with a white bounce card on right,
shallow depth of field focusing on the main element,
props: linen napkin, vintage silverware, wine glass with bokeh,
rich appetizing colors, delicate steam rising from hot elements,
Michelin restaurant menu photography quality
```

**示例效果：**

![精致料理摆盘](images/food-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 方形适合社交媒体 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 商业摄影 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为俯拍90度角，展现摆盘的几何美学
- 更换背景为白色大理石或质朴原木桌面
- 加入厨师双手正在摆盘的动态画面

**标签**: `#photography` `#food` `#finedining` `#plating` `#commercial`

## Prompt 2: 咖啡馆俯拍

> 精致咖啡馆桌面的平铺俯拍构图

**Prompt:**

```text
A stylish overhead flat-lay photograph of a café table arrangement,
artisan latte with detailed rosetta foam art as the centerpiece,
fresh croissant on parchment paper with flaky layers visible,
open magazine, vintage reading glasses, and a small succulent plant,
scattered coffee beans and a few crumbs for organic imperfection,
shot directly from above (90-degree angle) on a light marble tabletop,
soft diffused natural light creating minimal shadows,
warm neutral color palette: cream, caramel, soft brown, white,
Instagram lifestyle aesthetic with carefully curated casual feel
```

**示例效果：**

![咖啡馆俯拍](images/food-02.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 方形最适合俯拍 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 生活方式摄影 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为日式抹茶甜品搭配和风器皿

## Prompt 3: 亚洲街头小吃

> 充满烟火气的亚洲街头美食特写

**Prompt:**

```text
A mouthwatering close-up photograph of Asian street food being prepared,
sizzling dumplings in a bamboo steamer with steam billowing upward,
golden crispy skin with glistening oil droplets visible,
vendor's hands skillfully working with tongs in the background (out of focus),
dramatic side lighting from the cooking flame creating warm highlights,
authentic street food stall environment with wok and condiment bottles,
vibrant colors: chili red sauce, fresh green scallions, golden pastry,
shot at 85mm f/2 for intimate food portrait with creamy background blur,
editorial food photography meets documentary street style
```

**示例效果：**

![亚洲街头小吃](images/food-03.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 方形特写 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 纪实美食风 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为拉面特写，突出浓郁汤底和溏心蛋
- 换成泰国芒果糯米饭或越南河粉的热气腾腾场景
- 加入夜市背景灯光，用大光圈虚化为光斑

**标签**: `#photography` `#food` `#streetfood` `#asia` `#closeup`

## Prompt 4: 烘焙面包房

> 温暖面包房中新鲜出炉面包的诱人质感

**Prompt:**

```text
A warm inviting photograph of a rustic bakery display,
freshly baked artisan sourdough loaves with crackled golden crusts,
flour dust particles floating in a beam of morning window light,
wooden shelving and wicker baskets creating layered composition,
a baker's hand placing a fresh baguette on a cooling rack,
visible bread texture: open crumb structure on a sliced loaf in foreground,
warm color temperature emphasizing golden browns and cream tones,
shallow depth of field with sharp foreground loaf and soft background,
artisan bakery editorial photography with European countryside warmth
```

**示例效果：**

![烘焙面包房](images/food-04.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1536×1024 | 横版展现面包房环境 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 温暖商业风 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 聚焦单个面包的切面特写，展示气孔结构
- 改为法式甜点马卡龙的缤纷色彩排列
- 加入揉面团的过程画面，突出手工感

**标签**: `#photography` `#food` `#bakery` `#bread` `#warm`

## Prompt 5: 鸡尾酒艺术

> 精美调酒的光影艺术与液体质感

**Prompt:**

```text
A sophisticated cocktail photography shot in a moody upscale bar setting,
an elegant coupe glass containing a layered craft cocktail in jewel tones,
dramatic backlight creating luminous glow through the liquid,
ice sphere with visible clarity and internal fractures catching light,
garnish of dehydrated citrus wheel and fresh herb sprig,
condensation droplets on the glass surface,
dark moody background with subtle bokeh of bar bottles and warm ambient lights,
shot at 100mm macro f/2.8 for product-level detail,
deep shadows and rich highlights, luxury spirits advertising quality
```

**示例效果：**

![鸡尾酒艺术](images/food-05.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1536 | 竖版突出酒杯造型 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 暗调商业摄影 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为倒酒瞬间的动态画面，液体飞溅
- 换成热带Tiki风格鸡尾酒，色彩更加鲜艳
- 加入调酒师双手操作的画面，突出手工调制感

**标签**: `#photography` `#food` `#cocktail` `#bar` `#moody`
