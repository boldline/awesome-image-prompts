## Prompt 1: 科幻大片海报

> 赛博朋克末世题材的好莱坞式科幻电影海报

**Prompt:**

```text
A cinematic movie poster for a cyberpunk sci-fi blockbuster, a lone female protagonist in a glowing exoskeleton suit standing atop a crumbling skyscraper, looking down at a neon-drenched megacity engulfed in perpetual rain, massive holographic advertisements flickering in the fog below, dramatic orange-teal color grading with deep shadows, hero shot composition with the character occupying the upper third, volumetric light beams cutting through smog, film grain texture overlay, title space reserved at the bottom with metallic chrome font style area, IMAX presentation quality, 27x40 inch vertical poster proportions
```

**示例效果：**

![赛博朋克科幻海报](images/nyc-cinematic-01.jpg)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1536 | 竖版海报比例 2:3 |
| 风格 | Cinematic | 电影质感光影 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 将赛博朋克换为太空歌剧，背景改为星际战舰编队
- 将雨夜换为沙尘暴环境，呈现末日废土风格
- 增加双人构图，体现对峙或并肩作战关系

**标签**: `#movie-poster` `#sci-fi` `#cyberpunk` `#blockbuster`

## Prompt 2: 独立文艺电影海报

> 极简主义风格的文艺剧情片海报，强调意境

**Prompt:**

```text
A minimalist arthouse film poster for an indie drama about solitude and memory, a solitary figure seen from behind sitting on a wooden dock stretching into a vast still lake at twilight, the sky transitions from deep indigo to pale amber at the horizon, perfect mirror reflection in the water creating visual symmetry, extremely negative space with the figure occupying less than 10% of the frame, muted desaturated color palette with one warm accent (the figure's red scarf), no text overlay needed, Terrence Malick meets Edward Hopper visual poetry, melancholic atmospheric stillness, A24 studio aesthetic sensibility, fine art photography quality
```

**示例效果：**

![文艺电影海报](images/movie-poster-02.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1536 | 竖版海报比例 |
| 风格 | Artistic | 文艺质感 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为城市场景：雨中玻璃窗后模糊的人影
- 使用俯拍视角：人物躺在空旷草地中央
- 换为黑白摄影风格，仅保留一处彩色元素

**标签**: `#movie-poster` `#arthouse` `#minimalist` `#indie`

## Prompt 3: 恐怖片海报

> 心理恐怖类型的暗黑系电影海报

**Prompt:**

```text
A psychological horror movie poster, a Victorian-era porcelain doll sitting in a decayed rocking chair in the center of a dark abandoned nursery, one glass eye reflecting an impossible scene of a screaming face, cobwebs connecting the doll to the peeling wallpaper like neural networks, the room subtly warped with non-Euclidean geometry (walls slightly too long, doorframe at wrong angle), sickly green-yellow undertone lighting from an unseen source below, deep impenetrable shadows in every corner suggesting hidden presences, scratched film negative texture overlay, unsettling uncanny valley aesthetic, psychological dread rather than gore, James Wan meets Ari Aster visual language
```

![恐怖片海报](images/movie-poster-03.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1536 | 竖版海报比例 |
| 风格 | Cinematic | 暗黑电影风 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

## Prompt 4: 浪漫爱情片海报

```text
A romantic drama movie poster, a couple dancing on a rooftop terrace in 1960s Rome at golden hour, the man in a slim-cut dark suit and the woman in a flowing coral dress that catches the warm breeze, the Colosseum and Roman rooftops softly blurred in the bokeh background, warm golden sunset light wrapping around their silhouettes creating rim lighting halos, scattered string lights overhead forming soft circular bokeh, rose petals frozen mid-air around them, painterly soft focus with sharp details only on their intertwined hands, classic Hollywood romance color palette of gold amber and soft rose, Richard Linklater meets Wong Kar-wai romantic atmosphere, 35mm film warmth
```

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|

## Prompt 5: 动作冒险海报

> 高动态张力的动作冒险类型片海报

**Prompt:**

```text
An explosive action-adventure movie poster, dynamic diagonal composition with the hero mid-leap between two collapsing ancient temple structures over a river of molten lava below, the character wielding a glowing artifact in one hand while reaching for a vine with the other, massive stone guardian statues crumbling in the background with debris frozen in motion, fiery orange and deep jungle green complementary color scheme, speed lines and motion blur on peripheral elements while hero stays tack-sharp, helicopter searchlights cutting through volcanic ash clouds above, multiple depth layers creating a sense of overwhelming scale and danger, Indiana Jones meets Uncharted energy, adrenaline-pumping vertical composition with action flowing from top-right to bottom-left
```

**示例效果：**

![动作冒险海报](images/movie-poster-05.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1536 | 竖版海报比例 |
| 风格 | Cinematic | 高对比动态感 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |
