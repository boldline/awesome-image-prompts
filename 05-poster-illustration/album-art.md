## Prompt 1: 黑胶唱片封面

> 70年代经典摇滚风格的黑胶唱片封面设计

**Prompt:**

```text
A vintage vinyl record album cover in the style of 1970s progressive rock, a surreal impossible landscape where a grand piano melts and flows like liquid mercury down a staircase that ascends into a sky filled with giant clock gears and musical notation constellations, Roger Dean meets Salvador Dalí visual language, rich saturated colors with airbrush gradient technique (deep purple sky transitioning to burnt orange horizon), hand-painted quality with visible artistic texture, perfectly square 1:1 format (12-inch vinyl sleeve proportions), the surreal imagery tells a visual story that complements imagined music, gatefold inner sleeve art quality, band logo zone in top-left corner with custom 70s lettering style, no modern digital effects
```

**示例效果：**

![黑胶唱片封面](images/album-art-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 正方形专辑封面 |
| 风格 | Artistic | 复古绘画质感 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为迷幻摇滚风格：液态万花筒+荧光色彩
- 换为前卫摇滚：Hipgnosis摄影超现实风格
- 使用极简主义：单色背景+一个超现实物体

**标签**: `#album-art` `#vinyl` `#prog-rock` `#vintage`

## Prompt 3: 电子音乐封面

> 深度电子/Ambient风格的抽象视觉封面

**Prompt:**

```text
An abstract electronic music album cover for a deep ambient/techno release, a macro photograph of a single water droplet frozen at the exact moment of splashing on a vibrating speaker membrane, the ripple patterns in the water forming perfect geometric cymatics patterns (sacred geometry visible in the wave interference), the liquid appears to be made of bioluminescent fluid glowing deep teal and electric indigo against absolute black background, long exposure light trails from the vibrating droplets creating orbital paths, extreme shallow depth of field with razor-thin focus plane, the image exists in the liminal space between science photography and abstract art, Raster-Noton label aesthetic meets Ryoji Ikeda data visualization, clinical precision combined with organic fluid beauty, 3000x3000 pixel concept square format
```
