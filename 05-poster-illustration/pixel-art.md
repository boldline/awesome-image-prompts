## Prompt 1: 16-bit RPG城镇场景

> 经典16位时代JRPG风格的完整城镇场景，充满生活气息

**Prompt:**

```text
A detailed 16-bit pixel art scene of a bustling fantasy RPG market town in SNES/Super Famicom era quality, viewed from a top-down 3/4 perspective, the town square features: a central stone fountain with animated water sparkle pixels, merchant stalls with colorful awnings selling visible wares (potions in colored bottles, swords on racks, fruit baskets), cobblestone streets with 3-4 NPC characters in distinct outfits walking about their daily routines, a two-story inn with a wooden sign swinging, a blacksmith shop with visible orange glow from the forge, flower boxes on windowsills, a town wall visible in the background with a guard tower, each tile carefully placed with intentional dithering patterns for gradient effects on roofs and shadows, limited 64-color palette with distinct color ramps for each material (stone: 4 greys, wood: 4 browns, foliage: 4 greens), consistent pixel size throughout with no anti-aliasing, every detail placed with the precision of a professional pixel artist working within SNES hardware limitations, Chrono Trigger meets Secret of Mana town aesthetic
```

| 风格 | Pixel Art | 像素画风格 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为夜间版本：灯火通明的窗户+月光+萤火虫
- 换为冬季雪景版：屋顶积雪+冰冻喷泉+圣诞装饰
- 使用地牢/洞窟场景替代地面城镇
