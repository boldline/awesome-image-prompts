## Prompt 1: 浮空城市概念艺术

> 电影/游戏级别的奇幻浮空城市概念设计

**Prompt:**

```text
A breathtaking digital concept art painting of a sky archipelago civilization, multiple floating islands at varying altitudes connected by impossibly long suspension bridges made of woven golden chains and luminescent vines, the largest central island hosts a cathedral-city with Gothic spires that pierce through cloud layers, waterfalls pour from island edges evaporating into mist before reaching the cloud sea below, tiny airships with fabric sails navigate between the islands like a aerial merchant fleet, late afternoon golden hour light streaming horizontally through the scene creating god rays between islands, atmospheric perspective with distant islands fading to blue-grey silhouettes, birds wheeling in thermal currents around the falls, painterly digital brushwork with confident bold strokes visible at edges transitioning to refined detail at focal points, Makoto Shinkai color vibrancy meets Craig Mullins compositional mastery, suitable for AAA game cinematic or film pre-production
```

**示例效果：**

![浮空城市概念艺术](images/breaking-screen-01.jpg)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1536×1024 | 横版宽幅概念图 |
| 风格 | Digital Painting | 数字绘画质感 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为地下洞穴城市：巨大钟乳石柱上建造的垂直城邦
- 换为沙漠中的海市蜃楼城市：实体化的幻象建筑
- 使用暴风雨场景：雷电交加中的浮空城市防御战

**标签**: `#digital-art` `#concept-art` `#fantasy` `#environment`

## Prompt 3: 超现实主义数字绘画

> 梦境逻辑的超现实主义艺术创作

**Prompt:**

```text
A surrealist digital painting exploring the concept of time and memory, a vast desert landscape where enormous antique pocket watches grow from the sand like metallic flowers, their faces showing different times and some running backwards, the watch stems twist into tree-like branches bearing glass fruit containing miniature scenes of frozen memories (a birthday party, a first kiss, a funeral), in the center a giant hourglass lies on its side cracked open with its sand spilling out to form the surrounding desert itself (the desert IS the escaped time), the sky is a gradient from realistic sunset on one side to an exposed clockwork mechanism on the other revealing the universe as a constructed machine, impossible shadows that don't match their objects, color palette shifts from warm sepia (past) through desaturated present to cold blue (future) across the canvas, Dalí's technical precision with Remedios Varo's mystical imagination, high-resolution detail suitable for gallery print at large scale
```

**示例效果：**

![超现实主义数字绘画](images/digital-art-03.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 方形画布 |
| 风格 | Artistic | 超现实绘画 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为关于重力的超现实：同一场景中不同方向的引力
- 换为关于语言的超现实：文字从书中飞出变成实体物
- 使用关于身份的超现实：镜子迷宫中每面镜子反射不同年龄的自己

**标签**: `#digital-art` `#surrealism` `#conceptual` `#dreamscape`

## Prompt 5: 抽象能量体

> 纯视觉冲击力的抽象数字艺术创作

**Prompt:**

```text
An abstract digital art piece depicting the birth of a new universe, pure energy visualization without any recognizable objects, a central point of absolute white intensity radiating outward in all directions, the radiation transforms through states: nearest the center it's pure geometric fractals (Mandelbrot-set-like infinite complexity), the middle zone becomes flowing fluid dynamics in impossible colors (colors that seem to shift between warm and cool depending on how long you look), the outer edges dissolve into quantum foam texture of the fabric of spacetime itself, color progression from blazing white core through prismatic spectral bands to deep cosmic violet-black at edges, the entire piece vibrates with implied motion despite being static, inspired by scientific visualizations of magnetic fields, plasma dynamics, and particle collision events at CERN, no figurative elements whatsoever - pure abstraction that triggers emotional response through color and form alone, suitable for large-scale museum installation print (minimum 2 meters wide)
```

**示例效果：**

![抽象能量体](images/digital-art-05.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 方形构图 |
| 风格 | Artistic | 抽象表现 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为黑洞吸积盘：极端引力扭曲光线的可视化
- 换为量子纠缠：两个粒子系统的视觉关联表达
- 使用声音可视化：交响乐章转化为抽象色彩形态
