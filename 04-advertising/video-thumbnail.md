## Prompt 1: YouTube 科技评测封面

> 科技数码评测视频的高点击率封面，对比/悬念式构图

**Prompt:**

```text
A high-CTR YouTube thumbnail for a tech review video comparing two flagship smartphones, split composition with the latest iPhone on left and Samsung Galaxy on right, dramatic versus/battle theme with electric lightning bolt dividing the center, both phones shown at slight angles displaying their screens with visible camera arrays, dark smoky background with blue glow on left side and red glow on right side creating team color rivalry, exaggerated shocked expression reviewer face peeking from bottom center (small scale), bold contrasting neon colors for maximum feed standout, 1280x720 pixel dimensions, slightly over-saturated and high contrast optimized for small thumbnail size viewing, clean top area for 2-3 word text overlay
```

**示例效果：**

![YouTube 科技评测封面](images/video-thumbnail-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1536×864 | 16:9 YouTube 标准 |
| 风格 | Graphic + Photorealistic | 混合夸张风格 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为开箱视频封面，礼盒打开 + 光芒四射效果
- 替换为"XX天后"耐久测试，产品使用前后对比
- 采用排行榜/Top 10 格式，多产品网格布局

**标签**: `#advertising` `#video-thumbnail` `#youtube` `#tech-review` `#comparison`

## Prompt 2: 美食烹饪教程封面

> 美食频道烹饪教程的封面图，色香味俱全的食欲感设计

**Prompt:**

```text
A mouth-watering cooking tutorial YouTube thumbnail, hero shot of a perfectly plated homemade pasta dish with rich tomato sauce and fresh basil on a rustic ceramic plate, steam visibly rising captured with backlighting, dynamic action element of parmesan being grated mid-air with cheese shavings falling, warm kitchen environment slightly blurred in background with copper pots and herbs, food styled to magazine cover perfection with sauce drip on plate edge for authenticity, overhead 45-degree angle typical of food content, vibrant warm color palette emphasizing reds oranges and greens, right 30% slightly darker for white text overlay area, appetizing saturated colors that pop at thumbnail size, 16:9 landscape format
```

**示例效果：**

![美食烹饪教程封面](images/video-thumbnail-02.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1536×864 | 16:9 视频封面标准 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 美食摄影风格 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为甜品烘焙封面，切面展示层次 + 巧克力流淌

## Prompt 5: 直播预告封面

> 电商直播/活动直播的预告封面图，营造期待感与紧迫感

**Prompt:**

```text
A live stream announcement cover image for a beauty brand's product launch livestream event, glamorous studio setup with professional ring lights and camera equipment visible in background suggesting behind-the-scenes access, central display pedestal with a mysterious new product silhouette covered by a silk cloth with subtle shape hints, countdown timer graphic element (showing "LIVE 8PM") in upper-right corner with glowing neon effect, scattered rose gold confetti and sparkle particles creating celebration atmosphere, brand ambassador silhouette teased in background, luxurious color scheme of black, rose gold, and soft pink, "swipe up" or "set reminder" CTA area at bottom, square 1:1 format for multi-platform posting, building anticipation and FOMO through visual mystery and event staging
```

**示例效果：**

![直播预告封面](images/video-thumbnail-05.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 1:1 多平台通用方形 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic + Graphic | 活动视觉混合风格 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

- 改为游戏直播预告，赛博朋克风格 + 玩家ID展示
