## Prompt 1: 数据洞察讨论帖配图

> 用于引发讨论的数据可视化信息卡片

**Prompt:**

```text
A Twitter/X data insight discussion card, 1200x675 pixel format (16:9 ratio), a bold infographic showing a striking statistic "87% of startups fail in the first 3 years" with a large donut chart visualization in the center, dark charcoal background with a single accent color (electric coral) highlighting the key data point, clean modern sans-serif typography with high contrast white text, subtle grid pattern in the background adding depth, minimalist data visualization style inspired by The Economist and Bloomberg, designed to be immediately readable and shareable, sparks curiosity and drives engagement, professional thought leadership aesthetic
```

**示例效果：**

![数据洞察讨论帖配图](images/twitter-card-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1536×864 | Twitter 卡片 16:9 比例 |
| 风格 | Graphic Design | 信息图表风格 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 换成趋势对比图（两条线的增长/下降对比）
- 改为地图数据可视化（全球分布热力图）
- 时间轴数据（近十年某指标变化）

**标签**: `#social-media` `#twitter-card` `#data` `#infographic`
