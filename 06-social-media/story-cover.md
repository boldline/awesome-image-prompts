## Prompt 1: Instagram 精选集封面图标

> 统一风格的 Instagram Highlights 精选封面图标套组

**Prompt:**

```text
An Instagram Story Highlight cover icon set design, 1080x1920 vertical format showing a single icon optimized for circular crop display, a minimal line-art illustration of a coffee cup with steam wisps on a solid dusty rose pink background, clean single-weight stroke style (2px line weight feel) in cream white color, the icon is perfectly centered for circular cropping, ultra-simple recognizable silhouette design that reads clearly at 40px display size, consistent with a cohesive set aesthetic (same background color, same line style across Travel/Food/Work/Fitness/FAQ categories), no text or complex details, modern feminine lifestyle brand aesthetic
```

**示例效果：**

![Instagram精选集封面图标](images/story-cover-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 圆形裁切用正方形 |
| 风格 | Graphic Design | 极简图标设计 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 换成渐变彩色背景版本（每个类别不同渐变色）
- 改为填充式图标风格（实心剪影代替线条）
- 水彩手绘风格的精选封面

**标签**: `#social-media` `#story-cover` `#highlights` `#icon`

## Prompt 2: TikTok/抖音视频封面

> 抖音短视频的高点击封面图

**Prompt:**

```text
A TikTok/Douyin (抖音) video cover image, 1080x1920 vertical format (9:16), a dynamic food cooking scene showing hands tossing stir-fry ingredients in a wok with dramatic flames and flying vegetables frozen mid-air, shot from a slightly low angle to emphasize the dramatic action, warm kitchen lighting mixed with the orange glow of the flames, motion blur on flying food particles creating energy and movement, the lower third has a dark gradient overlay area for text and UI elements, vibrant saturated colors (red peppers, green scallions, golden oil splashes) against dark wok background, high-energy action shot that stops the scroll, professional food content creator production quality, vertical composition optimized for full-screen mobile viewing
```

**示例效果：**

![TikTok抖音视频封面](images/story-cover-02.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 768×1344 | 抖音/TikTok 9:16 竖屏 |
| 风格 | Photo | 动态摄影风格 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

- 变装视频封面（半屏前后对比的悬念构图）
