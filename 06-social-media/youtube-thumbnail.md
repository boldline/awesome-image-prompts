## Prompt 1: 科技测评缩略图

> 数码产品开箱/测评类视频的高点击率缩略图

**Prompt:**

```text
A high-CTR YouTube thumbnail for a tech review video, 1280x720 pixels (16:9), showing a person with an excited surprised expression holding up the latest smartphone with both hands, the phone screen glowing with exaggerated light rays, bold large text "WORTH IT?!" in 3D yellow block letters with black outline and drop shadow positioned in the upper right, split background with electric blue on left and vibrant orange on right creating visual tension, the reviewer is positioned in the left two-thirds wearing a casual hoodie, product close-up floating in the lower right with an arrow pointing to it, saturated punchy colors designed to pop against YouTube's white interface, professional studio lighting on the face with catchlights in eyes
```

**示例效果：**

![科技测评缩略图](images/youtube-thumbnail-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1536×864 | YouTube 缩略图 16:9 |
| 风格 | Graphic Design | 高饱和度设计 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 换成笔记本电脑测评（翻盖展示 + 性能跑分数据）
- 改为耳机评测（佩戴展示 + 音波可视化背景）
- 对比测评风格（两款产品左右 VS 分屏）
