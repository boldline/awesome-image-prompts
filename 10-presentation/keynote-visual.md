## Prompt 1: TED 风格概念视觉

> TED 演讲配合的全屏概念意象图

**Prompt:**

```text
A dramatic full-bleed keynote visual for a TED-style talk about human connection in the digital age, 16:9 cinematic widescreen. The image: two hands reaching toward each other across a digital void — one hand is organic and warm (realistic skin tones, soft lighting) and the other is composed of glowing data streams, code fragments, and constellation-like connected dots forming a hand shape. They almost touch in the center (Michelangelo's Creation of Adam reference), with a spark of warm golden light at the meeting point. Background: deep space-like darkness with subtle digital particles floating. The image is purely visual with zero text — the speaker provides all context. Designed for maximum emotional impact when projected on a massive stage screen, the visual metaphor is instantly readable from the back row of a 2000-seat auditorium, high contrast for stage lighting conditions, evocative rather than literal, inspires the audience to think deeply about the topic
```

**示例效果：**

![TED风格概念视觉](images/keynote-visual-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1536×864 | 16:9 宽屏 |
| 风格 | Conceptual / Cinematic | 概念电影风 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为气候变化主题（冰川与火焰的对比意象）
- 换成教育平等主题（一扇门通向不同世界）
- 使用抽象光影表达"突破边界"的概念

**标签**: `#presentation` `#keynote-visual` `#TED` `#conceptual` `#dramatic`

## Prompt 2: 产品发布会全屏展示

> Apple 风格产品发布会的全屏视觉

**Prompt:**

```text
A product launch keynote visual in Apple-style presentation aesthetic, 16:9 format, showcasing a new wearable tech device. The device (a sleek smart ring with titanium finish) floating in center-frame against a pure black background, dramatically lit from above creating a perfect highlight arc on the metallic surface. Subtle colored light reflections (deep purple and gold) playing across the polished surface suggesting premium materials. The device slightly rotated at a 3/4 angle to show both the exterior design and the inner sensor array. Micro-particles of light dust floating around it suggesting magic/innovation. No text whatsoever — purely the product as hero. Photography-level rendering quality: you can see the brushed metal texture, the subtle chamfered edges, the precision manufacturing. Lighting designed for pure black stage environments (OLED-friendly), creates the "one more thing" moment of reveal, product feels like it's hovering in space, the audience should audibly gasp
```

## Prompt 3: 白板动画/手绘风演讲

> 手绘风格的思维可视化演讲页面

**Prompt:**

```text
A whiteboard animation style keynote visual showing a complex business ecosystem, 16:9 format, designed to look like it was drawn in real-time during the talk. White/cream background (slightly warm, like a quality whiteboard). Hand-drawn style elements: a central hub labeled "Platform" drawn as a circle with radiating connections to 6 stakeholder groups (Creators, Consumers, Advertisers, Developers, Partners, Data), each represented by a simple sketch icon and connected by hand-drawn arrows showing value flow. Annotations scattered around in a casual handwriting font: "network effects!", "virtuous cycle", "moat" with underlines and circles for emphasis. Color coding: limited to 3-4 marker colors (blue for flow, red for emphasis, green for growth, black for structure). Small doodles and visual metaphors (flywheel, snowball, funnel). The overall effect looks like a brilliant strategist just sketched this during a conversation — organic, intellectual, and persuasive. Inspired by Dan Roam's "Back of the Napkin" visual thinking style
```

## Prompt 4: 数据故事全屏图

> 用一张震撼的数据可视化讲述故事

**Prompt:**

```text
A full-screen data storytelling keynote visual, 16:9 format, designed for a moment of dramatic revelation in a presentation about global internet adoption. The visual: a world map rendered in dark navy/black, with each country filled proportionally by glowing dots representing internet users — densely packed bright dots in connected nations, sparse in underserved regions. The dots pulse with a warm amber/gold glow creating a night-satellite-view-of-earth aesthetic. One massive number overlaid in the center-bottom in thin white display font: "3.7 Billion" with a subtitle "people still offline" — the dramatic twist (audience expected to see connectivity, instead sees the gap). A subtle gradient of hope: the dots at edges of connected regions are spreading outward like a growing organism. No axes, no chart chrome — just the pure data-as-art visualization. Designed to make the audience FEEL the data rather than just read it, emotional data visualization, suitable for a keynote moment before announcing an initiative to close the digital divide
```

| 风格 | Data Art / Storytelling | 数据艺术 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为气候变化数据（温度热力图时间推移）

## Prompt 5: Sketch Notes 视觉笔记

> 会议/演讲的实时视觉记录风格

**Prompt:**

```text
A sketchnote-style visual summary of a conference keynote speech about "The Future of Work", 16:9 landscape format, designed as if a graphic recorder captured the talk in real-time. Layout: organic and non-linear, with the main title "FUTURE OF WORK" in large hand-lettered bold text at top-center with a decorative banner. Content flows in clusters around the page: Cluster 1 (top-left): "Remote First" with drawings of a laptop, house icon, and world map with connection lines; Cluster 2 (top-right): "AI Collaboration" with a robot and human handshake sketch, bullet points in handwriting; Cluster 3 (bottom-left): "Skills > Degrees" with a torn diploma icon and growing plant metaphor; Cluster 4 (bottom-right): "4-Day Week" with a calendar sketch and happy stick figures. Connecting elements: arrows, dotted paths, and small transitional doodles between clusters. Typography: mixed hand-lettering styles (bold for headings, print for details, cursive for quotes). Colors: primarily black line work with 3 highlight colors (yellow marker for emphasis boxes, blue for connections, orange for key insights). The overall piece feels energetic, intellectual, and comprehensive — like attending the talk in visual form
```

**变体建议：**

- 改为技术架构讲解的视觉笔记
