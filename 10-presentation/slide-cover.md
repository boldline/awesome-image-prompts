## Prompt 1: 科技大会主题封面

> 技术峰会/开发者大会的震撼封面页

**Prompt:**

```text
A stunning tech conference presentation cover slide, 16:9 widescreen format (1920x1080). Dark navy to black gradient background with a subtle circuit board pattern fading into the edges. Central visual: an abstract 3D geometric structure made of glowing blue and cyan wireframe lines forming a neural network/constellation shape, floating with subtle particle effects around it. Title area: large bold sans-serif white text "THE FUTURE OF AI" with a thin horizontal line separator, subtitle in lighter weight "Building Intelligence at Scale", speaker name and date in small text at bottom. Design elements: subtle grid lines in the background, small glowing dots like stars scattered throughout, a single accent color (electric blue #0066FF) used sparingly for highlights, ultra-clean modern tech aesthetic, suitable for projection on large conference screens, inspired by Apple WWDC / Google I/O visual language
```

**示例效果：**

![科技大会主题封面](images/slide-cover-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1536×864 | 16:9 宽屏 |
| 风格 | Tech / Dark Theme | 科技暗色主题 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 将AI主题改为区块链/Web3（加入链状结构）
- 换成量子计算主题（薛定谔方程视觉化）
- 使用渐变霓虹色替代蓝色系

**标签**: `#presentation` `#slide-cover` `#tech` `#conference` `#dark-theme`

## Prompt 2: 创业公司路演封面

> 初创公司融资路演的第一页

**Prompt:**

```text
A startup pitch presentation cover slide, 16:9 format, clean and confident design. White background with generous whitespace creating a premium feel. Left-aligned layout: company logo placeholder (abstract geometric mark in coral/orange), large bold title "Revolutionizing Last-Mile Delivery" in dark charcoal black (system font aesthetic like Inter or SF Pro), a one-line subtitle in medium gray "AI-Powered Autonomous Delivery Network", thin accent line in brand coral color. Right side: a clean isometric illustration of a futuristic delivery drone/robot in the brand color palette (coral, white, soft gray), minimal and iconic rather than detailed. Bottom bar: "Series A | Confidential | Q1 2025" in small caps tracking. Overall aesthetic: Y Combinator demo day quality, Stripe/Linear inspired minimalism, professional yet startup-energetic, conveying innovation and trustworthiness simultaneously, no gradients or shadows - flat and modern
```

## Prompt 3: 学术/教育课件封面

> 大学课程或学术报告的专业封面

**Prompt:**

```text
A university lecture or academic presentation cover slide, 16:9 format, scholarly yet engaging design. Background: a deep forest green or navy academic color with subtle paper texture overlay (like aged parchment). Central composition: elegant serif typography for the title "Machine Learning Fundamentals" with a decorative thin rule above and below, course code "CS-4820 | Fall 2025" in small capitals, professor name and university affiliation below. Visual element: a tasteful gold/brass geometric illustration related to the topic (neural network diagram rendered in an elegant engraving/line art style), positioned as a background watermark at 15% opacity or as a contained illustration. Design references: Ivy League course aesthetics, academic journal covers, subtle nod to vintage scientific illustration, professional and intellectual without being stuffy, could work for both a Harvard lecture or an Oxford tutorial, balanced between tradition and modernity
```

## Prompt 5: 创意工作坊/活动封面

> 设计工作坊、创意活动的活泼封面

**Prompt:**

```text
A creative workshop or event presentation cover slide, 16:9 format, vibrant and energetic design that sparks creativity. Background: bright white with bold abstract paint strokes and geometric shapes scattered playfully — a large yellow circle, a coral pink brush stroke, scattered blue dots, a squiggly green line — creating a collage-like atmosphere of creative energy. Title: "Design Thinking Workshop" in a bold playful sans-serif font (slightly rounded, friendly), dark charcoal color for readability, slightly rotated 2-3 degrees for dynamism. Subtitle: "From Empathy to Prototype in 4 Hours" in a complementary lighter weight. Additional elements: small sketchy icons (lightbulb, pencil, sticky notes, arrows) drawn in a hand-illustrated doodle style, event details (date, location, facilitator) in a clean info block at the bottom. Overall aesthetic: creative confidence, inspired by IDEO/d.school workshop materials, Memphis design influence, accessible and inviting to non-designers, energetic without being chaotic, suitable for both screen and print
```

**示例效果：**

![创意工作坊活动封面](images/slide-cover-05.png)

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |

| 尺寸 | 1536×864 | 16:9 宽屏 |
| 风格 | Creative / Playful | 创意活泼风 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**
