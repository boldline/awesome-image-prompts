## Prompt 1: 科技行业年度报告

> 互联网/科技公司年度报告封面

**Prompt:**

```text
A professional annual report cover for a technology company, portrait A4 format (210x297mm). Design: clean modern layout with a bold geometric abstract visual — interlocking translucent shapes (circles, triangles, hexagons) in the company's brand colors (electric blue #0066FF and deep navy #0A1628), overlapping to create depth and suggest innovation/connectivity. The shapes are arranged in an upward-flowing composition suggesting growth and progress. Typography: "Annual Report 2025" in a modern geometric sans-serif (Euclid/Circular style) in white, large and confident, positioned in the upper third. Company name and logo cleanly placed at top-left. A subtle tagline "Connecting Tomorrow's World" in thin weight below the title. Bottom section: minimal footer with "Fiscal Year Ending December 2025" in small text. The overall design balances boldness with corporate professionalism, suitable for both digital PDF viewing and high-quality print production, communicates forward-thinking technology leadership without being flashy, inspired by annual reports from Alphabet, Microsoft, or Salesforce
```

**示例效果：**

![科技行业年度报告](images/report-cover-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1408 | A4 竖版 |
| 风格 | Corporate Tech | 科技企业风 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为金融公司年报（金色+深蓝，更保守）
- 换成创业公司年报（更大胆的配色和构图）
- 使用数据可视化图形作为封面主视觉

**标签**: `#presentation` `#report-cover` `#annual-report` `#tech` `#corporate`

## Prompt 2: 学术研究白皮书

> 行业白皮书或学术研究报告的封面

**Prompt:**

```text
A white paper cover design for an AI research publication titled "Large Language Models: Capabilities, Limitations, and Safety", portrait format. Clean academic-meets-industry aesthetic. Background: pure white with a single sophisticated visual element — an abstract neural network visualization rendered in fine gray lines with selective blue node highlights, positioned as a large background element at 30% opacity taking up the right 2/3 of the page. Text layout (left-aligned in the left third): institution/company logo at top, large title in dark serif font (Source Serif Pro style) with clear line breaks for readability, authors listed below in smaller sans-serif with affiliations, publication date and version number at bottom. A colored accent bar (3mm wide, gradient from blue to purple) runs along the left edge of the page providing a professional binding-edge detail. Footer: "Technical Report | v2.1 | March 2025" with a DOI-style reference number. The design communicates intellectual rigor and trustworthiness, looks credible enough to cite in academic papers, yet modern enough for a tech company's research division, inspired by papers from DeepMind, OpenAI, or Google Brain
```

## Prompt 3: 行业趋势报告

> 咨询公司/机构发布的行业洞察报告

**Prompt:**

```text
A trend report cover for "Global Digital Marketing Trends 2025" published by a consulting firm, portrait A4 format. Design approach: bold and visually striking to stand out in a crowded content market. Background: a rich dark gradient (deep purple #1a0533 to midnight blue) creating a premium backdrop. Central visual: an abstract data visualization art piece — flowing curves and streams of light in multiple colors (coral, gold, teal, magenta) representing different trend streams, converging and diverging dynamically, suggesting data flows, consumer journeys, and market movements. The streams have a fluid, organic quality — like aurora borealis made of data. Typography: report title in large white bold sans-serif (split across 2-3 lines for impact), year "2025" displayed extra-large as a design element (outline font style or gradient fill). Publisher logo and name at top in white, edition number ("8th Annual Edition") as a badge/stamp element. Bottom: a row of small partner/sponsor logos in white. The cover should make someone want to download this report immediately, balances analytical credibility with visual appeal, McKinsey/Deloitte quality with more creative flair
```

## Prompt 4: 政府/机构官方报告

> 政府机构或国际组织的正式报告封面

**Prompt:**

```text
A government or institutional report cover for "National Education Development Report 2025" (《2025年国家教育发展报告》), portrait A4 format, formal and authoritative. Design: structured and dignified with clear hierarchy. Background: clean white with a subtle watermark pattern of the national emblem or institutional seal at very low opacity (5%) centered. Top section: organization name and logo centered, followed by a thick colored band (Chinese red #C41E3A or institutional blue #003366) containing the report title in white text, both Chinese and English. Middle section: a full-width photograph (campus scene with students and modern educational facilities, warm and aspirational) contained in a clean rectangular frame with a thin border. Below the photo: key information in structured layout — report period, issuing department, publication date, document number. Bottom: institutional footer with address and official markings. Typography: combination of Song-style (宋体) for Chinese headers and clean sans-serif for English, hierarchical sizing clearly communicating document importance. The overall design conveys authority, trustworthiness, and public accountability — clearly an official document at first glance
```

## Prompt 5: 创意/设计行业作品集封面

> 设计公司或创意机构的年鉴/作品集封面

**Prompt:**

```text
A creative agency portfolio or design yearbook cover, portrait format with a slightly non-standard size (200x260mm square-ish) for a premium printed publication. Design: maximally creative while maintaining sophistication. The cover features a bold experimental typographic treatment — the word "CREATE" in massive display type spanning the full cover, but each letter is filled with a different texture/image: C (marble texture), R (gradient mesh in vibrant colors), E (circuit board pattern), A (botanical illustration), T (starfield/space), E (watercolor splash). The letters overlap slightly and break the edges of the cover (bleed off the page). Background: off-white premium uncoated paper texture. Additional elements: the year "2025" debossed/embossed effect in bottom corner, studio name in minimal small text, a foil-stamped accent detail (gold foil on one element). The design itself IS the portfolio piece — demonstrating the studio's range through the cover alone. Print-production aware: shows understanding of special finishes (spot UV, foil, deboss), paper choice matters, designed to be held and admired as a physical object, collectible quality
```

**示例效果：**

![创意设计作品集封面](images/report-cover-05.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1280 | 接近方形比例 |
| 风格 | Creative / Experimental | 创意实验风 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为摄影年鉴（全幅照片+极简文字）
- 换成建筑事务所作品集（几何结构+材质）
- 使用纯字体排版的瑞士风格极简封面
