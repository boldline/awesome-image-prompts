## Prompt 1: 问题陈述页

> Pitch Deck 中定义市场痛点的关键页面

**Prompt:**

```text
A startup pitch deck "Problem" slide, 16:9 format, designed to make investors immediately feel the pain point. Dark background (charcoal #1a1a2e) for dramatic impact. Large section label "THE PROBLEM" in coral red accent color at top-left with a subtle warning icon. Main content: three pain point statements arranged vertically, each with a large bold statistic on the left (e.g., "73%", "$2.4T", "4.5hrs") in white display font, followed by a concise pain description in gray ("of small businesses fail due to cash flow issues"). Between each stat, a thin subtle separator line. Right side: a single powerful visual — a simplified illustration or icon showing frustration/broken process in the accent color (a cracked/broken gear, or tangled lines). Bottom: a subtle callout box with the killer stat "Market inefficiency costs $2.4 Trillion annually" highlighted. Design philosophy: create urgency, use contrast (big numbers vs. small text), dark theme conveys seriousness, Sequoia Capital pitch deck aesthetic, every element serves the narrative of "this problem is big and unsolved"
```

![问题陈述页](images/pitch-deck-01.png)

**变体建议：**

- 改为用户痛点旅程图（日常场景中遇到的挫折）
- 换成竞品对比的"现有方案不足"角度
- 使用客户真实引言作为痛点佐证

## Prompt 2: 解决方案页

> 展示产品核心解决方案的页面

**Prompt:**

```text
A pitch deck "Solution" slide, 16:9 format, transitioning from the problem's dark mood to an optimistic, bright design. Clean white background with brand color accents (vibrant blue #2563EB). Title: "OUR SOLUTION" in brand blue at top-left, with a subtitle "AI-Powered Financial Intelligence for SMBs". Center layout: a hero product mockup — a clean dashboard UI shown on a laptop/tablet screen at a slight 3D perspective angle, displaying a friendly analytics interface with graphs trending upward. Around the mockup, 3 key benefit callouts positioned with connecting dotted lines to relevant parts of the UI: "Predict cash flow 30 days ahead" (crystal ball icon), "Automate invoicing & collections" (automation icon), "Real-time financial health score" (heart/pulse icon). Each callout is a small card with icon + short text. Bottom: a single-line value proposition in bold "Turn financial chaos into clarity — in under 5 minutes." The overall feel shifts from the problem slide's darkness to relief and clarity, professional SaaS product presentation, investor-ready quality
```

## Prompt 3: 市场规模页

> TAM/SAM/SOM 市场分析的经典页面

**Prompt:**

```text
A pitch deck "Market Size" slide showing TAM/SAM/SOM analysis, 16:9 format, compelling and clear. White background with strategic use of a single brand color (emerald green #059669 for money/growth association). Title: "MARKET OPPORTUNITY" at top-left. Main visual: three concentric circles (target/bullseye diagram) in graduating shades of green — largest outer circle labeled "TAM: $340B" (Total Addressable Market) in lightest shade, middle circle "SAM: $45B" (Serviceable Addressable Market), inner/darkest circle "SOM: $4.5B" (Serviceable Obtainable Market) with "Year 1 Target: $50M" as a bright accent point at center. To the right of the circles: brief 1-line explanations for each layer. Below the diagram: a horizontal bar showing market growth rate "Growing 24% CAGR (2023-2028)" with a source citation (Gartner/McKinsey style). The design makes the market look large enough to be exciting but shows focused execution through the SOM. Clean, data-forward, no fluff, designed to answer "Is this market big enough?" in 3 seconds
```

## Prompt 4: 增长数据/Traction 页

> 展示业务增长曲线和关键里程碑

**Prompt:**

```text
A pitch deck "Traction" slide showcasing impressive growth metrics, 16:9 format, designed to build investor confidence. Light gray background (#FAFAFA) for a clean data-forward feel. Title: "TRACTION" with a small rocket icon in brand purple (#7C3AED). Hero element: a large area chart showing MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) growth over 18 months with a beautiful smooth upward curve (hockey stick shape), filled with a gradient from light purple to transparent, clear Y-axis labels ($0 to $500K), subtle gridlines marking each quarter. Above the chart: four metric boxes in a row showing key numbers — "500+ Customers" / "$420K MRR" / "15% MoM Growth" / "< 3% Churn" — each with a small green up-arrow indicating positive trend. Below the chart: a timeline of key milestones marked with dots on a horizontal line — "Product Launch", "First 100 Customers", "Enterprise Deal", "Profitability" with dates. Design conveys momentum and inevitability, numbers are the star, clean and verifiable feel, avoids looking "too designed" — lets the data speak, inspired by the best Stripe/Figma early pitch decks
```

## Prompt 5: 融资需求/Ask 页

> 明确融资金额和资金用途的收尾页

**Prompt:**

```text
A pitch deck "The Ask" slide — the fundraising request page, 16:9 format, clear and confident. Clean white background with brand accent (deep blue #1E40AF). Large centered headline: "Raising $8M Series A" in bold dark text, creating immediate clarity. Below: "To accelerate growth and expand into 3 new markets" in medium gray. Main content: a horizontal allocation bar (like a stacked progress bar) showing fund usage breakdown with distinct colors — Engineering/Product 40% (blue), Sales & Marketing 30% (green), Operations 20% (amber), Reserve 10% (gray). Below the bar: each allocation has a one-line detail ("Hire 15 engineers, ship v3.0 platform"). Right section: key milestones the funding will achieve within 18 months — "5,000 customers", "$5M ARR", "3 new markets", "Series B ready" as a simple checklist with checkboxes. Bottom: "Let's build the future of financial intelligence together" in italics as a closing statement, followed by contact information and a subtle CTA. The slide balances specificity (showing you have a plan) with confidence (showing you'll deliver), not apologetic about asking for money, clear use of funds justification
```
