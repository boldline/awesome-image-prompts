## Prompt 1: 护肤效果对比

> 护肤产品使用前后的皮肤状态对比，适合美妆/护肤品牌

**Prompt:**

```text
A professional skincare before-and-after comparison image in split-screen format, left side labeled "Before" showing close-up of skin with visible uneven texture, dullness, and minor blemishes under clinical lighting, right side labeled "After" showing the same skin area now with a healthy radiant glow, smooth texture, and even tone, identical lighting setup and camera angle on both sides for authentic comparison, clean white clinical background, subtle dividing line with a small arrow pointing from left to right, dermatology documentation style photography, realistic and believable improvement without over-editing, professional skincare brand results documentation
```

**示例效果：**

![护肤效果对比](images/before-after-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1280×720 | 横版便于左右对比 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 皮肤细节真实 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为上下对比布局适配手机竖屏
