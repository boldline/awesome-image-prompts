## Prompt 1: 家居装饰场景

> 家居产品融入温馨客厅环境，适合家居品牌

**Prompt:**

```text
A warm lifestyle scene of a modern Scandinavian living room featuring a bouclé fabric armchair with a hand-knit throw blanket draped over it, a ceramic table lamp glowing softly on a walnut side table, potted monstera plant in the corner, late afternoon golden hour sunlight streaming through sheer linen curtains casting long shadows, product is naturally integrated into the room rather than staged, cozy hygge atmosphere, shot on 35mm f/2.8 for natural depth of field with focus on the featured furniture piece, editorial interior photography style
```

**示例效果：**

![家居装饰场景](images/lifestyle-scene-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1280×720 | 横版适合详情页Banner |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 室内光影真实感 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 更换为日式侘寂风格，使用原木和陶器
- 将时间改为清晨，营造明亮清新的氛围
- 添加一只猫咪蜷缩在椅子上增加生活气息

**标签**: `#ecommerce` `#lifestyle` `#home-decor` `#interior`
