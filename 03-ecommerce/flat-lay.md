## Prompt 1: 时尚穿搭平铺

> 服饰搭配的俯拍展示，适合电商服装套装页面

**Prompt:**

```text
A top-down flat lay of a complete fashion outfit arrangement on a clean white linen surface, including a folded cashmere sweater, high-waisted jeans, leather belt, minimalist gold jewelry, sunglasses, and a crossbody handbag, items arranged with intentional spacing and geometric alignment, soft natural daylight from a north-facing window, subtle fabric textures visible, muted earth-tone color palette with one accent color pop, negative space on the right for text overlay, ultra-sharp overhead product photography at exactly 90 degrees
```

**示例效果：**

![时尚穿搭平铺](images/flat-lay-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 方形适合社交媒体 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 真实质感面料 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 将白色亚麻底替换为浅灰水泥地面，增加工业感
- 加入一杯拿铁和杂志作为生活化点缀
- 更换为全黑色系穿搭，搭配深色木质背景
