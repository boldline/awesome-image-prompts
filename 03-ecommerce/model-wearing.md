## Prompt 1: 街头潮流穿搭

> 街头风格服饰的城市场景模特展示

**Prompt:**

```text
A streetwear fashion e-commerce model shot, young male model in his early 20s wearing an oversized graphic hoodie in washed grey, baggy cargo pants with multiple pockets, chunky white sneakers, and a crossbody utility bag, standing confidently on an urban street with graffiti-covered walls and industrial architecture in the background (slightly blurred), natural late afternoon city light with warm tones, candid yet intentional pose — one hand adjusting the hoodie drawstring, weight shifted to one leg, full-body shot showing complete outfit from head to toe, clothing details clearly visible: print quality, fabric draping, fit silhouette, street style photography with authentic urban energy, shot at f/2.8 for subject separation from background
```

**示例效果：**

![街头潮流穿搭](images/model-wearing-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1536 | 竖版全身展示 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 街拍时尚摄影 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为女性街头穿搭：工装裤+紧身上衣+厚底鞋
- 换为夜间霓虹灯场景增加视觉冲击力
- 添加滑板、耳机等潮流配件
