## Prompt 1: 极简主义盒装

> 简约风格产品包装展示，适合现代品牌

**Prompt:**

```text
A minimalist product packaging design mockup of a rectangular rigid box with magnetic closure, matte white exterior with a single debossed logo in center, box shown at a three-quarter angle with lid partially open revealing tissue paper inside, placed on a clean light grey seamless background, soft studio lighting with a single key light from upper left creating gentle shadows, packaging dimensions clearly readable, ultra-clean Muji-inspired aesthetic, professional packaging photography for brand presentation, sharp focus on material texture and edge details
```

**示例效果：**

![极简主义盒装](images/packaging-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 方形 |
| 风格 | Photorealistic | 材质纹理清晰 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 将白色换为牛皮纸色，增加自然质朴感
- 添加烫金logo替代压印效果
- 旁边放置产品本身形成开箱组合

**标签**: `#ecommerce` `#packaging` `#minimal` `#box`
