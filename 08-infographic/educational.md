## Prompt 1: 人体解剖示意图

> 医学/生物教育风格的解剖标注图，适合教材配图

**Prompt:**

```text
An educational anatomy infographic showing the human digestive system, clean medical illustration style with a simplified body outline in neutral skin tone, digestive organs highlighted in distinct colors (mouth in pink, esophagus in light red, stomach in orange, liver in dark red, small intestine in yellow, large intestine in brown), each organ connected by labeled leader lines to descriptive text boxes on the sides, text boxes contain: organ name in bold, brief function description, key fact or measurement, numbered sequence showing the digestion pathway (1-7), clean white background with subtle blue medical grid pattern, professional medical textbook aesthetic, anatomically proportionate but stylized for clarity (not photorealistic), color-coded legend at bottom, title "The Digestive System" in clear educational typography, suitable for biology textbook, health education poster, or patient information leaflet
```

**示例效果：**

![人体解剖示意图](images/educational-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 768×1024 | 竖版适合人体展示 |
| 风格 | Medical Textbook / Clean Illustration | 医学教材风格 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为心血管系统或呼吸系统
- 使用卡通风格适配儿童教育读物
- 添加放大细节框展示微观结构（如绒毛）

**标签**: `#infographic` `#educational` `#anatomy` `#medical` `#biology`

**Prompt:**

```text
An educational nutrition infographic breaking down a balanced meal plate, top-down view of a plate divided into sections following dietary guidelines (vegetables 40%, grains 30%, protein 20%, dairy 10%), each section filled with beautiful illustrated food items in that category, surrounding the plate: nutrient callout cards connected by lines to specific foods, each card showing: nutrient name (Vitamin C, Iron, Fiber, Protein), daily value percentage, health benefit in one line, color-coded food groups using standard nutrition colors (green for vegetables, brown for grains, red/pink for protein, blue for dairy), additional glass of water illustrated beside the plate, calorie counter badge showing total meal calories, clean modern health-magazine style with white background, friendly rounded typography, small food pyramid reference in corner, suitable for nutrition education, wellness blog, dietitian handout, or school cafeteria poster
```
