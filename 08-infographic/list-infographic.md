## Prompt 1: Top 10 排行榜

> 经典的十大排名榜单，适合社交媒体传播

**Prompt:**

```text
A visually engaging "Top 10 Productivity Apps for 2024" ranked list infographic. Vertical layout with items numbered 1-10, each item displayed as a card row containing: large ranking number (bold, colored gradient from gold #1 to silver lower ranks), app icon placeholder, app name in bold, one-line description, and a star rating indicator. Visual hierarchy emphasizing top 3 (larger cards, gold/silver/bronze accent colors), items 4-10 slightly more compact. Alternating subtle background bands (white and cream) for readability, cohesive color scheme with deep purple header and golden accent for numbers, decorative crown icon on #1, bookmark-worthy design quality with polished finish, branded header section with title, category tag, and date, footer with "Save & Share" prompt and source attribution, tall vertical Pinterest-optimized format
```

**示例效果：**

![Top 10 排行榜](images/list-infographic-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 768×1536 | 竖版长图适合榜单排列 |
| 风格 | Editorial / Ranked | 编辑排名风格 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为 Top 5 精简版适配 Instagram Story
- 使用横向条形图风格（越高排名条越长）
- 添加价格/评分等对比维度

**标签**: `#infographic` `#list` `#top10` `#ranking`

## Prompt 2: 实用技巧清单

> 实用 Tips 合集，一图收藏多条建议

**Prompt:**

```text
A practical tips list infographic: "7 Daily Habits of Highly Effective Designers". Each tip displayed in its own contained section with: numbered badge (1-7), relevant flat-style icon, tip title in bold, and 2-line actionable description. Tips arranged vertically with subtle separator lines between sections, visual rhythm created through consistent spacing and alignment. Color-coded number badges cycling through a harmonious palette (coral, teal, amber, violet, sky blue, mint, pink), clean white background with light geometric pattern texture, modern sans-serif typography (DM Sans or Poppins style), motivational header with lightbulb icon, "Pro tip" highlighted callout box for the most impactful suggestion, footer with author credit and hashtag suggestions, portrait format optimized for saving and sharing on LinkedIn or Instagram
```

**示例效果：**

![实用技巧清单](images/list-infographic-02.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1344 | 竖版适合技巧列表展示 |
| 风格 | Clean / Modern | 清爽现代风格 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为"5 个"精简版减少信息负荷
- 使用卡片网格布局代替纯列表

**标签**: `#infographic` `#list` `#tips` `#productivity`

## Prompt 5: 数字统计清单

> 以数字为核心的要点清单，数据说话

**Prompt:**

```text
A numbers-driven list infographic: "2024 Content Marketing — 8 Stats You Can't Ignore". Each list item led by a large bold statistic number (e.g., "4.2B", "73%", "10x") as the visual anchor, followed by the insight text in smaller type explaining what the number means. Items stacked vertically with generous spacing, each statistic number in a different vibrant color from a curated palette (electric blue, coral red, emerald green, amber, violet), subtle icon or mini illustration beside each stat reinforcing the concept, alternating alignment (number left/right) for visual rhythm, dark charcoal background with the colorful numbers creating visual pop, thin accent lines separating items, modern editorial magazine layout feel, source citations in small superscript notation, bold typographic header with year badge, suitable for content marketing blog or newsletter header image, portrait format with strong vertical scanning flow
```

**示例效果：**

![数字统计清单](images/list-infographic-05.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1344 | 竖版适合数字清单展示 |
| 风格 | Editorial / Dark | 深色编辑风格 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**
