## Prompt 1: 年度市场报告图表

> 适合年度报告的专业统计图表组合

**Prompt:**

```text
A professional annual market report infographic with multiple statistical charts arranged in a structured grid layout. Hero section at top: bold headline "Global SaaS Market Report 2024" with key finding subtitle. Main content area divided into 4 quadrants: (1) stacked bar chart showing revenue by segment over 5 years, (2) pie/donut chart breaking down market share by top 5 players, (3) line chart with shaded area showing growth trajectory and forecast zone (dashed line for projections), (4) horizontal bar chart ranking top markets by region. Corporate color palette: deep navy primary, steel blue secondary, gold accent for highlights, clean white background with subtle grid lines, professional serif headlines (Georgia style) paired with sans-serif body text, data source citations in small text at bottom, A4 portrait format suitable for PDF report page
```

**示例效果：**

![年度市场报告图表](images/statistical-01.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1344 | 竖版 A4 比例适合报告页 |
| 风格 | Corporate / Report | 企业报告专业风格 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为单一大图表 + 详细标注的专项分析
- 使用深色主题适配科技行业报告
- 添加同比/环比变化箭头标注

**标签**: `#infographic` `#statistical` `#report` `#corporate`

## Prompt 2: 调查结果可视化

> 将问卷调查数据转化为易读的统计图

**Prompt:**

```text
A survey results infographic presenting "Employee Satisfaction Survey 2024" findings in an engaging visual format. Large sample size indicator at top (N=2,500 with people icon array), main findings displayed as: horizontal progress bars showing satisfaction by category (work-life balance 78%, compensation 65%, growth opportunities 72%, team culture 85%), each bar color-coded from red (low) through yellow to green (high), highlight callout boxes for top insight and area of concern, demographic breakdown pie charts (by department, tenure) in sidebar, Net Promoter Score gauge meter prominently displayed, quotation bubble with representative employee feedback, friendly yet professional design with rounded elements, teal and coral accent colors on clean white, suitable for HR presentation or internal newsletter, portrait format
```

**示例效果：**

![调查结果可视化](images/statistical-02.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1344 | 竖版适合调查数据展示 |
| 风格 | Friendly Professional | 友好专业风格 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 改为客户满意度调查（CSAT/NPS）
- 使用表情符号图标代替进度条增加亲和力
- 添加同比数据对照显示趋势变化

**标签**: `#infographic` `#statistical` `#survey` `#hr`

## Prompt 3: 社交媒体数据图

> 适合社交平台分享的单一统计亮点

**Prompt:**

```text
A social-media-optimized statistical infographic designed for maximum shareability and impact. Single powerful statistic as the centerpiece: "92% of marketers say video is important for their strategy" — the number "92%" rendered in enormous bold gradient typography (taking up 50% of visual space), surrounded by a circular animated-style progress ring, supporting context in smaller text below, 3 mini supporting stats in icon+number format at bottom (like "2.5x more engagement", "78% ROI increase", "45% faster growth"), vibrant eye-catching color scheme with electric blue background and white/yellow text for contrast, subtle motion-implied design elements (speed lines, glow effects), Instagram square format optimized for feed visibility at small thumbnail size, brand watermark area in corner
```

**示例效果：**

![社交媒体数据图](images/statistical-03.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1024 | 方形适合社交媒体发布 |
| 风格 | Bold / Social Media | 社交媒体醒目风格 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 制作轮播图系列（每张一个数据点）

**标签**: `#infographic` `#statistical` `#social-media` `#bold`

## Prompt 4: 行业趋势折线图

> 展示多年趋势变化的精美折线图

**Prompt:**

```text
A beautifully designed trend line chart infographic showing "E-commerce Growth Trends 2019-2025". Main visualization: smooth curved line chart with 3 overlaid lines (mobile commerce in purple, desktop in blue, total in gold), shaded area beneath each line with gradient transparency, clear data point markers at each year with value labels, notable events annotated with callout pins (e.g., "COVID impact" spike marker in 2020), y-axis showing revenue in billions, x-axis showing years, forecast section (2024-2025) distinguished with dashed lines and lighter shading, trend insight text box highlighting key finding ("Mobile surpassed desktop in 2022"), minimalist Scandinavian-inspired design with generous white space, muted sophisticated color palette, thin elegant typography (Inter or Helvetica Neue), subtle dot grid background, landscape format ideal for blog header or report figure
```

**示例效果：**

![行业趋势折线图](images/statistical-04.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1536×1024 | 横版适合趋势线展示 |
| 风格 | Scandinavian / Minimal | 北欧极简风格 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 添加置信区间阴影带表示预测不确定性
- 使用对数坐标轴展示指数增长
- 改为面积图（area chart）增加视觉厚重感

**标签**: `#infographic` `#statistical` `#trend` `#line-chart`

## Prompt 5: 人口统计图标阵列

> 用图标矩阵直观表示比例和分布

**Prompt:**

```text
A pictogram/icon array statistical infographic showing demographic or proportion data: "1 in 4 adults experience mental health challenges annually". Visual representation using a 10x10 grid of person icons (100 total), where 25 icons are highlighted in a distinct color (teal) and the remaining 75 in light gray, creating an immediate visual proportion understanding. Additional supporting statistics below using smaller icon arrays or waffle charts for related data points, each with its own color coding. Clean flat design with plenty of breathing room, accessible color choices with sufficient contrast, empathetic and respectful visual tone (no negative imagery), explanatory caption and data source clearly cited, color legend explaining what each shade represents, suitable for health awareness campaign or NGO report, portrait format with comfortable reading flow
```

**示例效果：**

![人口统计图标阵列](images/statistical-05.png)

**参数说明：**

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| 尺寸 | 1024×1344 | 竖版适合图标矩阵排列 |
| 风格 | Flat / Accessible | 扁平无障碍风格 |
| 模型 | GPT-Image-2 | 推荐 |

**变体建议：**

- 使用其他图标形状（圆点、方块、自定义图形）
- 改为华夫饼图（waffle chart）方格展示
- 添加动画暗示（部分图标有渐变效果）

**标签**: `#infographic` `#statistical` `#pictogram` `#accessible`
